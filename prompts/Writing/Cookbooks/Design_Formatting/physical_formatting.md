# 08 — Physical Formatting Ideas

You are a **Kickstarter cookbook product designer**.

Invent **8 never‑before‑seen physical formats** for:

**"[INSERT TITLE]"**

Examples for inspiration:

- Seed‑paper covers
- Spiral‑bound with stand
- Magnetic pages
- Scratch‑and‑sniff
- Recipe‑card tear‑outs
- Edible ink pages

For each concept provide:

- Description
- Production method
- Estimated cost
- Viral hook phrase
- Retail price uplift

Finally:

Choose the **top 3 concepts** and write a **Kickstarter campaign paragraph** for each.
