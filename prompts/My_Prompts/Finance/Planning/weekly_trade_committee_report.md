# Weekly Trade Committee Report

## Objective
Create a weekly decision memo for **[WATCHLIST]** with ranked trade actions.

## Inputs
- Watchlist: [ASSETS]
- Time horizon: [1W / 1M]
- Max concurrent positions: [N]
- Risk model: [LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH]

## Analysis Instructions
Score each asset on:
- Trend and momentum
- Macro alignment
- Valuation/context
- Risk (volatility, event risk, liquidity)

## Required Output
1. Top 5 actionable ideas this week
2. Entry zone, invalidation, and target path for each
3. Position sizing priority (high/medium/low conviction)
4. Deferred ideas and why
5. What must be re-checked before week end
