# Dividend Tracker Weekly Sync

**Schedule:** Every Monday — 8:00 AM
**Category:** Finance
**Tools Required:** Notion, Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~5 minutes

## Prompt

Open my Notion dividend tracking database. For each dividend-paying stock in the portfolio:
1. Search for any announced dividend changes (raises, cuts, or special dividends) from the past 7 days
2. Calculate forward annual yield based on most recent share price
3. Flag any stocks with a payout ratio above 80% or dividend coverage concerns

Update the Notion database with current yield values and add a "⚠️ Review" tag to any flagged stocks. Then write a brief summary of this week's dividend health — total projected annual income, any changes, and stocks worth monitoring.
