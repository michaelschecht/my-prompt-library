# Inbox Zero Morning Triage

**Schedule:** Daily — 7:30 AM
**Category:** Operations
**Tools Required:** Gmail
**Estimated Time:** ~4 minutes

## Prompt

Scan my Gmail inbox for unread messages from the last 18 hours. Categorize each as:
- 🔴 Action Required (needs reply or decision today)
- 🟡 Review (read but no immediate action)
- 🔵 FYI / Digest (newsletters, notifications)
- 🗑️ Archive (safe to dismiss)

For 🔴 items: draft a one-line suggested reply or next step. For investor or partner emails, flag with [HIGH VALUE]. Output a morning triage list sorted by priority. Do not move or delete anything — just produce the triage report so I can work through it efficiently.
