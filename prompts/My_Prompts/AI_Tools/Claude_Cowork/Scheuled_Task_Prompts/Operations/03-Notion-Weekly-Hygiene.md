# Notion Weekly Hygiene

**Schedule:** Every Saturday — 10:00 AM
**Category:** Operations
**Tools Required:** Notion
**Estimated Time:** ~6 minutes

## Prompt

Do a weekly hygiene pass on my Notion workspace. Check:
1. Any tasks marked "In Progress" for more than 10 days — flag for review
2. Database entries missing required fields (status, owner, due date)
3. Pages in "Inbox" or unsorted sections that haven't been organized
4. Any stale portfolio tracking rows (stocks with price data older than 5 days)
5. Project pages without an update in the last 7 days

Output a Notion Health Report: what's stale, what's missing, and the top 5 cleanup actions. Prioritize items related to AX Platform and the investment portfolio. Suggest which items I can batch-fix in under 15 minutes.
