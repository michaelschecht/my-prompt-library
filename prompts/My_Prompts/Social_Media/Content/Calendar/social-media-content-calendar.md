# Social Media Content Calendar Generator

## Purpose
Create a structured social media content calendar including post types, topics, and publishing times based on marketing goals and audience demographics.

## Instructions
You are a professional social media strategist with expertise in content planning and calendar management. Please create a detailed social media content calendar for me based on the following information:

### Basic information

Please provide the following information so I can create a customized content calendar:

- Platform: [Social media platform where the content is to be published, such as: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.]
- Content goals: [Brand awareness, community building, lead generation, sales conversion, etc.]
- Target audience: [Description of main audience groups, including age, interests, behaviors, etc.]
- Brand voice: [brand’s tone and personality, such as: professional, casual, humorous, educational, etc.]
- Key products/services: [Products or services that need to be promoted]
- Important dates: [product releases, promotions, industry events, holidays, etc.]
- Post frequency: [ideal number of posts per week/month per platform]
- Content type preference: [pictures, videos, stories, live broadcasts, blogs, Q&A, etc.]
- Existing Content Assets: [Content assets that can be reused or adapted]
- Industry topic: [Related industry topics, trends or keywords]
- Competitor Performance: [Competitors’ content strategy and performance on social media]

### The content calendar I will provide will include:

#### 1. Monthly Overview

- Monthly content topics and highlights
- Key marketing activities and milestones
- Content distribution and balance
- Platform specific strategies

#### 2. Detailed weekly plan

For weekly offers:

- Daily content suggestions
- Platform assignment for each post
- Content type and format
- Best time to post

#### 3. Detailed post plan

For each suggested post, provide:

- Post topic and purpose
- Suggested content framework
- Main information and selling points
- Call-to-action (CTA) suggestions
- Related tag suggestions
- Guidelines for visual elements

#### 4. Content Category Assignment

Recommended mix of content types, such as:

- Educational content (%)
- Promotional content (%)
- User-generated content (%)
- Entertainment content (%)
- Interactive/participatory content (%)
- Behind the scenes/brand story (%)

#### 5. Seasonal and trending content suggestions

- Industry-related seasonal content
- Trend riding opportunities
- Real-time marketing potential points

#### 6. Content Reuse Strategy

- Cross-platform content adaptation suggestions
- How to split long content into multiple posts
- Evergreen content reuse program

#### 7. Engagement Strategy

- Interactive tips and question suggestions
- Community management schedule
- User-generated content solicitation program

#### 8. Performance Tracking Framework

- Key performance indicator (KPI) recommendations
- Analysis and reporting structure
- A/B testing opportunities

#### 9. Content Creation Workflow

- Content preparation schedule
- Suggestions on approval process
- Estimation of resource requirements

Please provide a specific, practical content plan that includes actual post examples and creative concepts, not just a generic template. The calendar should be both strategic and straightforward to execute, helping me build a consistent social media presence and achieve my marketing goals.

## Output Format
Provide the output in a clear, well-structured format with appropriate headings and sections.
