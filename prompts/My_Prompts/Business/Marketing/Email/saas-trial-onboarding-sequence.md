# SaaS Free Trial Onboarding Email Sequence

## Purpose
Design a strategic, multi-day email sequence designed to onboard new users of a SaaS product during their free trial, educate them on core features, and ultimately drive conversion to a paid subscription.

## Instructions
Act as an expert SaaS email marketer. Develop a comprehensive 14-day free trial onboarding sequence. The emails should focus on guiding the user to their "Aha! moment" as quickly as possible, overcoming common objections, and providing clear value before asking for the sale.

Specify the B2B SaaS product (e.g., project management software, marketing automation tool, CRM), the length of the trial, and the primary action that correlates with long-term retention.

## Output Format
Structure the onboarding sequence as follows:
1. **Sequence Strategy & Goals**: Define the ideal user journey, the primary goal (conversion), and the critical path to value.
2. **Email 1: Welcome & Immediate Action (Day 1)**: Subject line, preview text, and full body copy focusing on one simple, high-impact task.
3. **Email 2: The Core Feature Deep-Dive (Day 3)**: Highlighting the product's primary value proposition.
4. **Email 3: Overcoming the Blank Slate/Friction (Day 6)**: Providing templates, resources, or examples.
5. **Email 4: Social Proof & Case Study (Day 10)**: Building trust through a successful customer story.
6. **Email 5: The Soft Pitch & Urgency (Day 13)**: Reminding them the trial is ending and outlining paid benefits.
7. **Email 6: The Final Push/Offer (Day 14)**: The hard close with a potential incentive or clear call-to-action to upgrade.

## Example
**Input:**
Product: A B2B social media scheduling and analytics tool.
Trial Length: 14 days.
Primary Action: Connecting at least 3 social profiles and scheduling the first post.

**Output:**
### 1. Sequence Strategy & Goals
**Goal:** Convert 15% of trial users to the "Pro" plan.
**Critical Path:** Getting users to connect their accounts immediately, as users who connect 3+ accounts are 80% more likely to convert.

### Email 1: Welcome & Immediate Action (Day 1)
**Subject:** Welcome! Let's save you 5 hours this week.
**Preview:** The first step to putting your social media on autopilot...
**Body:**
Hi [Name],
Welcome to [Product Name]! We're thrilled to have you.

To get the most out of your 14-day trial and start saving time immediately, you need to do one thing today:
**Connect your top 3 social media profiles.**

[Button: Connect Profiles Now]

It takes less than 60 seconds. Once connected, you can schedule your entire week's content in under an hour.
Need help? Reply to this email, I'm here.

Cheers,
[Signature]

*(Continues for the remaining 5 emails)*
