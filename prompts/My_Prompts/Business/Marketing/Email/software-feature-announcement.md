# Software Feature Announcement Email

## Purpose
Craft a compelling product update email to announce a highly anticipated new feature or major integration to existing software users, driving immediate adoption and excitement.

## Instructions
Act as a Product Marketing Manager for a B2B software company. Write a single, high-impact feature announcement email. The email must clearly explain *what* the feature is, *why* the user should care (the benefit), and *how* they can start using it immediately. The design should be skimmable and visually engaging.

Provide the software type, the name of the new feature, the primary user pain point it resolves, and the call-to-action (CTA).

## Output Format
Structure the feature announcement email as follows:
1. **Subject Line Options**: Provide 3 distinct subject line variations (e.g., direct, benefit-driven, curiosity-inducing).
2. **Preview Text**: A compelling secondary hook.
3. **The Hook (Introduction)**: Acknowledge the user's problem and introduce the solution.
4. **The Value Proposition (The "Why")**: 2-3 bullet points detailing the specific benefits and time/money saved.
5. **The "How-To" (Visual/Steps)**: A brief explanation or suggestion for a GIF/image showing the feature in action.
6. **Call to Action (CTA)**: Clear, actionable button text.
7. **Secondary Resources**: Links to help documentation or a release webinar.

## Example
**Input:**
Software: Customer Support CRM.
New Feature: AI-Powered Suggested Replies.
Pain Point: Support agents spend too much time typing out repetitive answers to common questions.
CTA: "Try AI Replies Now"

**Output:**
### 1. Subject Line Options
- Direct: Introducing AI-Powered Suggested Replies
- Benefit: Cut your ticket resolution time in half ⚡
- Curiosity: Your new AI support assistant is here.

### 2. Preview Text
Let our AI draft your responses so your team can focus on complex issues.

### 3. The Hook
Hi [Name],
We know that speed is everything in customer support. But typing out the same answer to "Where is my order?" 50 times a day is exhausting for your team and slows down response times.

Today, that changes. Say hello to **AI-Powered Suggested Replies**.

### 4. The Value Proposition
By analyzing your past tickets and knowledge base, our new AI instantly drafts accurate, personalized responses for your agents to review and send.
- **Save Hours Daily:** Reduce average handling time (AHT) by up to 40%.
- **Ensure Consistency:** Keep your brand voice uniform across all agents.
- **Zero Setup Required:** It works out of the box using your existing data.

*(Continues with the How-To, CTA, and Secondary Resources)*
