# Cloud Service Upsell Campaign

## Purpose
Develop a targeted email campaign to upsell existing customers on a higher-tier plan or add-on service within a cloud computing, hosting, or infrastructure platform.

## Instructions
Act as a customer lifecycle marketer for a cloud services provider. Create a 3-part email upsell campaign aimed at existing, active users who are approaching the limits of their current plan or who would significantly benefit from an enterprise-tier feature (e.g., enhanced security, dedicated support, higher compute limits).

The tone should be helpful and consultative, positioning the upgrade as a natural next step for their growing business rather than a hard sales pitch. Specify the current plan, the target upgrade, and the specific behavioral trigger that initiates the campaign.

## Output Format
Provide the upsell campaign in the following format:
1. **Campaign Trigger & Segmentation**: Define exactly when a user receives this sequence (e.g., "Reached 80% of storage capacity") and the target segment.
2. **Email 1: The 'Heads Up' & Value Alignment**: Notifying them of the trigger and introducing the benefits of scaling.
3. **Email 2: The Feature Spotlight & Case Study**: Highlighting a specific premium feature that solves a growing pain point, backed by customer success.
4. **Email 3: The Offer/Direct Consultation**: Providing a clear upgrade path, potentially with a time-sensitive discount or an invitation to speak with an account manager.

## Example
**Input:**
Current Plan: Standard Cloud Hosting ($50/mo).
Target Upgrade: Pro Managed Cloud with Auto-Scaling and DDoS Protection ($200/mo).
Trigger: Customer's server CPU usage has hit 90% three times in the last month.

**Output:**
### 1. Campaign Trigger & Segmentation
**Trigger:** 3 instances of >90% CPU utilization within 30 days.
**Segment:** E-commerce and media sites on the Standard plan.

### Email 1: The 'Heads Up' & Value Alignment
**Subject:** Your traffic is growing (and so is your CPU usage)
**Body:**
Hi [Name],
Congratulations on the recent growth at [Company Name]!

We noticed that your server's CPU usage has hit 90% a few times over the past month. While your current Standard plan has served you well, running near capacity can lead to slow load times for your users during traffic spikes.

To ensure your site remains lightning-fast as you scale, it might be time to look at our Pro Managed Cloud tier, which includes seamless auto-scaling.

[Button: View Pro Plan Benefits]

Let us know if you have any questions about handling your increased traffic.

Best,
The [Company] Team

*(Continues for emails 2 and 3)*
