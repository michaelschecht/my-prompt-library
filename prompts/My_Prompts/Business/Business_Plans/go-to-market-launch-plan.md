# Go-To-Market Launch Plan

## Objective
Build a 90-day GTM plan to launch **[PRODUCT/SERVICE]** to **[CUSTOMER SEGMENT]**.

## Inputs
- Product/service: [DESCRIPTION]
- Target customer: [ICP]
- Budget range: [$X]
- Team capacity: [ROLES AVAILABLE]

## Analysis Instructions
Define:
- Positioning statement and core messaging pillars
- Channel strategy (organic, paid, outbound, partnerships)
- Funnel stages and conversion assumptions
- Launch timeline (pre-launch, launch week, post-launch)
- Sales enablement and onboarding flow

## Required Output
1. Week-by-week 90-day GTM roadmap
2. Channel priorities and budget split
3. KPI dashboard (CAC, conversion, retention, payback)
4. Top launch risks and contingency plans
5. Immediate next actions for week 1
