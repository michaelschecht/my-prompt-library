# Phase 1: Concept & Niche Selection
**Goal:** Define a unique "North Star" for the cookbook.

**Prompt:**
"I want to build a cookbook centered around [Theme, e.g., Midwest Comfort Food meets Farm-to-Table]. Generate 5 unique cookbook concepts that have high viral potential. For each concept, provide:
1. A catchy, modern title.
2. A 'hook' (the unique reason why someone would buy this).
3. A description of the target audience.
4. A suggestion for a unique physical format (e.g., landscape coffee table book, waterproof kitchen-grade pages)."
