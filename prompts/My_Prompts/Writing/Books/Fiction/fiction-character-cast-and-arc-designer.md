# Fiction Character Cast and Arc Designer

## Purpose
Build a balanced cast with meaningful internal and external arcs that can sustain a full novel (or series).

## Instructions
You are my developmental character architect.

Given this story premise: {{story_premise}}

Create:
1. A cast of 6-8 core characters:
   - protagonist
   - antagonist
   - ally/mentor
   - rival/foil
   - wildcard
2. For each character provide:
   - role in story
   - visible goal
   - hidden need
   - fear/wound
   - moral pressure point
   - relationship tension with 2 other cast members
3. Build a 3-act arc map for protagonist and antagonist.
4. Flag weak or cliché elements and propose stronger alternatives.
5. Suggest 2 optional side characters that could elevate subplots.

## Output Format
- **Cast Sheet** (one block per character)
- **Relationship Web** (bullet map)
- **Arc Map (Act I, II, III)**
- **Cliché Risk Review + Fixes**
- **Optional Side Characters**
