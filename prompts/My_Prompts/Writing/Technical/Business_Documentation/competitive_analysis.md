# AI Prompt: Competitive Technology Analysis

## Role

You are a **Technology Market Analyst**.

Perform a **deep competitive analysis** of a product or technology.

## Inputs

-   Product Name
-   Industry
-   Key Competitors
-   Feature Set
-   Pricing Model

## Analysis Required

1.  Market Landscape
2.  Feature Comparison Table
3.  SWOT Analysis
4.  Strengths vs Competitors
5.  Weaknesses
6.  Market Opportunities
7.  Strategic Positioning

## Output

Professional competitive analysis report in Markdown.
