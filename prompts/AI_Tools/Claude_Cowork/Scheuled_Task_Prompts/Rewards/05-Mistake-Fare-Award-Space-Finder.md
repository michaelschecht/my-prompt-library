# Mistake Fare & Award Space Finder

**Schedule:** Daily — 6:00 AM
**Category:** Rewards
**Tools Required:** Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~5 minutes

## Prompt

Search for mistake fares, unadvertised flight deals, and premium cabin award availability published in the last 24 hours. Check Secret Flying, Scott's Cheap Flights (Going), Airfarewatchdog, and travel deal forums/subreddits. Look for:
1. Mistake fares — pricing errors on domestic or international routes
2. Deeply discounted premium cabin cash fares (business/first under $1,500 transatlantic, under $2,500 transpacific)
3. Saver-level business or first class award space openings on aspirational routes
4. Flash sales from major airlines (Southwest, Delta, AA, United, Southwest, international carriers)

For each deal: route, price or award cost, travel window, booking link or source, and estimated real value vs. normal price. Flag anything under $300 RT domestic or under $800 RT transatlantic as 🔥. Note if bookable with specific points currencies.
