# Airline Miles Transfer & Sweet Spot Alert

**Schedule:** Every Tuesday & Friday — 7:30 AM
**Category:** Rewards
**Tools Required:** Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~6 minutes

## Prompt

Search for current airline miles transfer bonuses and award booking sweet spots. Check The Points Guy, View from the Wing, One Mile at a Time, and FlyerTalk. Look for:
1. Active transfer bonuses from Amex, Chase, Citi, Capital One, or Bilt to airline partners (e.g. "30% bonus transferring to Air France/KLM")
2. Any newly discovered or restored award sweet spots in airline programs
3. Mileage sale announcements (buy miles at a discount)
4. Partner redemption deals — e.g. using United miles on ANA, using Avios on AA

For each find: program name, transfer ratio or deal terms, expiration date, and a practical example redemption (e.g. "Business class JFK→NRT for 88k miles vs. normal 110k"). Flag anything expiring within 7 days as 🔥 URGENT.
