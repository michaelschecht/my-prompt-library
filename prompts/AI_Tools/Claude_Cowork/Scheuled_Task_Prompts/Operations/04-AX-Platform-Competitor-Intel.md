# AX Platform Competitor Intel

**Schedule:** Every Wednesday — 9:00 AM
**Category:** Operations
**Tools Required:** Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~7 minutes

## Prompt

Search for recent news and updates from companies in the enterprise AI agent security and collaboration space. Monitor for:
- Product launches or feature announcements from direct competitors
- New funding rounds or M&A activity in the AI agent governance space
- Enterprise customer win announcements
- Job postings that signal strategic direction (e.g. a competitor hiring IAM engineers)
- Any coverage mentioning "Agent Sprawl", "MCP security", or "AI governance platform"

Produce a competitive intel brief: 3–5 notable moves this week, a "threat level" assessment (Low/Medium/High) for each competitor, and one strategic recommendation for Jacob based on the landscape shift. Keep it crisp — this is boardroom-ready intel.
