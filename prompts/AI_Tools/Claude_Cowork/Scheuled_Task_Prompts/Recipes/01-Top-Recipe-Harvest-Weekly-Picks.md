# Top Recipe Harvest — Weekly Picks

**Schedule:** Every Saturday — 9:00 AM
**Category:** Recipes
**Tools Required:** Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~10 minutes

## Prompt

Search for this week's most popular and trending recipes across major cooking sites: Serious Eats, NYT Cooking, Bon Appétit, Food52, The Kitchn, AllRecipes, and Smitten Kitchen. Look for:
1. Most-saved or most-viewed recipes published or featured this week
2. Seasonal ingredients currently at peak (check what's in season for Central California / San Joaquin Valley)
3. One "project recipe" — something ambitious and impressive for a weekend cook
4. One quick weeknight recipe under 30 minutes
5. One recipe featuring an unusual technique or ingredient worth learning

For each recipe: title, source site, key ingredients, estimated time, difficulty level, and a one-sentence "why this one" note. Then fetch the full recipe text and ingredients list for each and save them as individual markdown files to the local workspace at: ~/recipes/YYYY-MM-DD/[recipe-slug].md

Include the source URL at the top of each saved file.
