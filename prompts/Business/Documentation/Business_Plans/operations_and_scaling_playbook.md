# Operations and Scaling Playbook

## Objective
Create an operating plan to scale **[BUSINESS]** from **[CURRENT STAGE]** to **[NEXT STAGE]**.

## Inputs
- Current stage: [STAGE]
- Target stage: [STAGE]
- Team capacity: [CURRENT TEAM]
- Growth target: [X CUSTOMERS / X REVENUE]

## Analysis Instructions
Define:
- Core workflows (delivery, support, quality, fulfillment)
- Required systems/tooling stack
- Hiring order and role priorities
- Bottlenecks and automation opportunities
- Capacity planning and service-level targets

## Required Output
1. Operating model blueprint
2. 6-month scale milestones
3. Hiring/delegation plan
4. Weekly ops KPI dashboard
5. Top failure points and safeguards
