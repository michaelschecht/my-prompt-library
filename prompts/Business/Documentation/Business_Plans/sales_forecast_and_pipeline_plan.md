# Sales Forecast and Pipeline Plan

## Objective
Create a realistic 12-month sales forecast and pipeline execution plan for **[BUSINESS]**.

## Inputs
- Sales model: [INBOUND / OUTBOUND / HYBRID]
- Average deal size: [$X]
- Sales cycle length: [DAYS]
- Team capacity: [HEADCOUNT / QUOTA]

## Analysis Instructions
Build:
- Pipeline stages and conversion assumptions
- Segment-based revenue forecast
- Lead-gen targets by channel
- Capacity vs quota model
- Gap-to-target recovery paths

## Required Output
1. Monthly forecast summary and assumptions
2. Quarterly pipeline coverage targets
3. Leading indicators to monitor weekly
4. Gap-to-goal analysis + corrective actions
5. 90-day sales execution priorities
