# Business Plan Strategy Brief

## Objective
Create a complete business plan for **[BUSINESS IDEA]** targeting **[CUSTOMER SEGMENT]** in **[GEOGRAPHY]**.

## Inputs
- Business concept: [DESCRIPTION]
- Target customer: [ICP]
- Market region: [COUNTRY/REGION]
- Business model preference: [B2B/B2C/SUBSCRIPTION/SERVICE]
- Stage: [IDEA / MVP / EARLY REVENUE]

## Analysis Instructions
Develop:
- Problem definition and current alternatives
- Product/service value proposition
- TAM/SAM/SOM assumptions
- Competitive landscape and differentiation
- Revenue model and pricing direction
- Go-to-market strategy and channel plan
- Team and operating model
- 12-month milestones and execution sequence

## Required Output
1. One-page executive summary
2. Business model and growth strategy
3. Top assumptions and key risks
4. Risk mitigation plan (top 5)
5. Go / no-go recommendation with rationale
