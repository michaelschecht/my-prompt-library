# Pitch Deck Templates

Professional templates for creating compelling pitch decks for investors, customers, and partners.

## Available Templates

### 1. Investor Pitch Deck Template
**File:** `Investor_Pitch_Deck_Template.md`

**Use for:**
- Fundraising presentations (Seed, Series A, B, etc.)
- Investor meetings
- Demo days and pitch competitions
- VC firm presentations
- Angel investor pitches

**Deck Length:** 12-15 slides  
**Presentation Time:** 15-20 minutes + Q&A

**Key Features:**
- Complete 13-slide structure
- Problem-solution-market-traction flow
- Financial projections guidance
- Investor Q&A preparation
- Stage-specific variations (pre-seed to Series B+)

**Best for:** Raising capital from VCs, angels, or strategic investors

```

│ I need to pitch...                                        │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ 💰 Investors for funding       → Investor Pitch Deck     │
```

### Decision Tree

**Are you raising money?**
→ Yes → **Investor Pitch Deck Template**
→ No → Continue...

**Is the pitch under 5 minutes?**
→ Yes → **Quick Pitch Template**

## Pitch Deck Best Practices

### Universal Rules (All Pitch Types)

**Content:**
- ✅ One idea per slide
- ✅ Lead with the problem, not the solution
- ✅ Use real data and metrics
- ✅ Tell a story, not just facts
- ✅ Show momentum and traction
- ✅ End with a clear call-to-action

**Design:**
- ✅ Minimal text per slide (headlines + bullets)
- ✅ High-quality visuals
- ✅ Consistent branding
- ✅ Readable fonts (30pt+ for body text)
- ✅ Clean, professional layout
- ✅ Use charts instead of tables

**Delivery:**
- ✅ Practice until natural
- ✅ Know your numbers cold
- ✅ Engage with audience
- ✅ Leave time for questions
- ✅ Have backup slides ready
- ✅ Bring energy and passion

### Common Mistakes

❌ **Too much text** on slides  
❌ **Reading from slides** instead of presenting  
❌ **No clear problem** statement  
❌ **Weak differentiation** from competitors  
❌ **Unrealistic projections** or claims  
❌ **Going over time** limit  
❌ **Forgetting the ask** (investor) or CTA (sales)  
❌ **Poor design** or inconsistent branding

## Industry-Specific Tips

### Tech/SaaS
- Emphasize scalability and recurring revenue
- Show product screenshots/demo
- Highlight tech differentiators
- Include ARR, MRR, churn metrics

### Consumer/Retail
- Focus on brand and customer love
- Show product in action
- Emphasize unit economics
- Include CAC, LTV, retention

### Healthcare/Biotech
- Address regulatory pathway
- Show clinical validation
- Emphasize team expertise
- Include market access strategy

### Fintech
- Security and compliance upfront
- Address regulatory landscape
- Show transaction metrics
- Emphasize trust and reliability

### B2B/Enterprise
- Focus on ROI for customers
- Show integration capabilities
- Emphasize security and scale
- Include enterprise logos

## Pitch Types by Stage

### Pre-Seed
- **Focus:** Problem, solution, team, vision
- **Traction:** Prototype, early users, validation
- **Ask:** $50K-$500K for MVP and first customers
- **Template:** Investor Pitch Deck (simplified)

### Seed
- **Focus:** Product-market fit, early traction
- **Traction:** Revenue or user growth, key hires
- **Ask:** $500K-$3M for scaling
- **Template:** Investor Pitch Deck (full)

### Series A
- **Focus:** Proven model, ready to scale
- **Traction:** Strong growth metrics, unit economics
- **Ask:** $2M-$15M for growth
- **Template:** Investor Pitch Deck (detailed financials)

### Series B+
- **Focus:** Market leadership, path to exit
- **Traction:** Revenue scale, market position
- **Ask:** $15M+ for market domination
- **Template:** Investor Pitch Deck (strategic vision)
