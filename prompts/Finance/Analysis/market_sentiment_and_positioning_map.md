# Market Sentiment & Positioning Map

## Objective
Build a sentiment and positioning map for **[ASSET OR INDEX]** over **[TIMEFRAME]** to support buy/sell/wait decisions.

## Inputs
- Asset/index: [ASSET OR INDEX]
- Timeframe: [INTRADAY / 1W / 1M / 3M]
- Trading style: [SWING / POSITION / LONG-TERM]
- Risk tolerance: [LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH]

## Analysis Instructions
Evaluate and summarize:
- Retail vs institutional sentiment signals
- Fear/greed regime and trend changes
- Positioning crowding risk (overbought/oversold, squeeze risk)
- Contrarian vs trend-following interpretation
- Whether sentiment supports continuation or reversal

## Required Output
1. Sentiment score (0–100, bearish → bullish)
2. Positioning risk level (Low/Medium/High)
3. What sentiment implies for near-term price behavior
4. Decision stance: **Follow trend / Fade move / Wait**
5. Invalidation triggers that would change the stance
