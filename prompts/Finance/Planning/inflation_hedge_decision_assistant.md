# Inflation Hedge Decision Assistant

## Objective
Select the best inflation-hedge mix for the current macro regime.

## Inputs
- Inflation view: [RISING / STICKY / FALLING]
- Timeframe: [4–12 WEEKS / 3–6 MONTHS]
- Risk tolerance: [LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH]

## Analysis Instructions
Compare hedge choices:
- Gold
- Energy exposure
- Broad commodities
- Inflation-linked bonds
- Defensive equity sectors

## Required Output
1. Current inflation pressure assessment
2. Recommended hedge mix and weighting logic
3. Entry approach (staggered vs immediate)
4. False-signal risks and tradeoffs
5. Action plan for next review cycle
