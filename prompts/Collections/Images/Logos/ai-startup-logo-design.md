# AI Startup Logo Design

## Purpose
Generate logo ideas for an innovative new AI technology startup, blending futuristic elements with a clean, trustworthy design.

## Instructions
Design a logo that represents artificial intelligence. Use abstract geometric shapes, subtle references to neural networks or the brain, and a modern typography (if a wordmark is included). Keep the design scalable and versatile for app icons and websites. The colors should be professional, such as deep blue or vibrant purple, symbolizing intelligence and future technology.

## Output Format
Provide the prompt for an AI image generator to create the logo, specifying the visual style, central motif, color scheme, and background.

## Example
**Image Generation Prompt:** "A minimalist vector logo for an AI startup. The central icon is an abstract geometric brain made of interconnected nodes, sleek and modern. Deep blue and electric purple gradient. The word 'AuraAI' in a clean, futuristic sans-serif font below the icon. Solid white background, flat vector style, scalable, corporate branding."
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** Minimalist Vector, Startup Branding, Clean
