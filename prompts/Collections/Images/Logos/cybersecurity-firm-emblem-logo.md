# Cybersecurity Firm Emblem Logo

## Purpose
Create a strong, authoritative emblem or badge-style logo for an IT cybersecurity and defense firm.

## Instructions
Design an emblem or crest-based logo that conveys absolute security, digital protection, and technical expertise. Incorporate motifs such as a high-tech shield, a digital lock, an eagle, or an eye. The aesthetic should be modern, perhaps slightly tactical or cybernetic, using colors like stark black, metallic silver, or neon blue/green.

## Output Format
Provide the image generation prompt with instructions on the emblem shape, technical elements, and authoritative tone of the logo.

## Example
**Image Generation Prompt:** "A modern, high-tech emblem logo for a cybersecurity firm. The central icon is a sleek, metallic shield interwoven with digital circuit board lines and a central glowing blue lock. Authoritative, secure, and tactical aesthetic. Black background with silver and neon blue accents. Vector graphic, symmetrical, badge style."
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** Modern Emblem, High-Tech Badge, Vector
