# SaaS Pricing Tier Graphic

## Purpose
Create a visual mockup of SaaS (Software as a Service) pricing tables to use as inspiration for web design or marketing assets.

## Instructions
Design a clean, modern SaaS pricing page graphic showing three distinct tiers (e.g., Basic, Pro, Enterprise). Highlight the middle "Pro" tier. The graphic should show features with checkmarks, pricing, and call-to-action buttons in a professional web-design aesthetic.

## Output Format
Provide the image generation prompt with specific details on the layout, highlighting, and design style of the pricing tiers.

## Example
**Image Generation Prompt:** "A modern UI/UX web design mockup of a SaaS pricing page featuring three subscription tiers. Clean white cards on a soft gray background. The middle 'Pro' tier is highlighted with a vibrant blue border, a 'Most Popular' badge, and a prominent call-to-action button. Minimalist, professional business aesthetic, high quality, dribbble style."
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** UI/UX Design, Web Layout, Minimalist
