# AI Neural Network Illustration

## Purpose
Generate a visually striking illustration of an AI neural network for use in tech blogs, presentations, or website headers.

## Instructions
Create an illustration depicting a complex artificial neural network. The visual should focus on glowing nodes and interconnected data pathways, conveying a sense of advanced machine learning and artificial intelligence. Use a modern, high-tech color palette.

## Output Format
Provide the prompt for an AI image generator (like Midjourney or DALL-E) to create the graphic, along with recommended aspect ratio and style keywords.

## Example
**Image Generation Prompt:** "A futuristic 3D illustration of an artificial neural network. Glowing cyan and neon purple nodes connected by luminous data streams on a dark background. Concept of deep learning, artificial intelligence, and big data. High resolution, 8k, digital art, cybernetic aesthetic."
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Style:** 3D Render, Futuristic, Abstract
