# HealthTech Service Awareness Campaign

## Purpose
Develop a social media campaign to increase awareness and trust for a new HealthTech service, targeting patients or healthcare providers.

## Instructions
Act as a healthcare communications specialist. Create an awareness-focused social media campaign for a HealthTech service (e.g., telemedicine platform, remote patient monitoring, mental health app). The campaign must prioritize trust, privacy, and tangible health outcomes. Choose either Facebook (for patient targeting) or LinkedIn (for provider targeting).

Provide the service description, the primary audience (patients vs. providers), and the key health benefit. Ensure the tone is empathetic, compliant with healthcare marketing standards, and scientifically backed.

## Output Format
Present the campaign in the following format:
1. **Campaign Foundation**: Objective, audience persona, and compliance considerations (e.g., HIPAA).
2. **Content Themes**: 3-4 main content pillars (e.g., Patient Stories, Medical Expertise, How-It-Works).
3. **Sample Content Matrix**: Provide 5 distinct post concepts including:
   - Visual Description
   - Ad Copy
   - Call to Action (CTA)
4. **Community Engagement Strategy**: How to handle comments, questions, and sensitive medical inquiries on social media.

## Example
**Input:**
Service: Virtual mental health counseling for busy professionals.
Audience: Patients (Targeting via Facebook/Instagram).
Key Benefit: Access to licensed therapists within 24 hours from home.

**Output:**
### 1. Campaign Foundation
**Objective:** Drive awareness and website visits for initial consultations.
**Audience Persona:** Stressed professionals aged 25-45 seeking convenient therapy options.
**Compliance:** No specific patient data shared; all testimonials anonymized and compliant with privacy regulations.

### 2. Content Themes
1. **Relatability:** Validating the stress of modern work culture.
2. **Accessibility:** Emphasizing the "24-hour access" feature.
3. **Expertise:** Short video tips from licensed therapists.

*(Continues with sample content and engagement strategy)*
