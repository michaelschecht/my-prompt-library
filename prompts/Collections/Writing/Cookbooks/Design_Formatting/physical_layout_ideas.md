# Phase 7: Unique Physical Formatting
**Goal:** Design the physical object for maximum impact.

**Prompt:**
"I am publishing a physical version of my cookbook. Provide 5 unique formatting ideas for physical distribution that differentiate it from a standard hardcover. Consider:
1. Spiral-bound lay-flat designs with 'tabbed' sections for different seasons.
2. A 'Box Set' format where each recipe is an individual high-quality card.
3. Incorporating sustainable materials (e.g., seed-paper covers that can be planted).
4. A 'Mini-Zine' companion book for quick 15-minute meals."
