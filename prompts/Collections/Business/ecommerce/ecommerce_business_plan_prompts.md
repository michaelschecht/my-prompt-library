# LLM Prompt Set for E-commerce Business Plan Generation

This document contains a set of prompts for generating a comprehensive e-commerce business plan using a Large Language Model (LLM).

## 1. Executive Summary

*   **Prompt:** "Write a concise and compelling executive summary for an e-commerce business. The business is [briefly describe business concept, products, and target market]. The summary should highlight the company's mission, vision, and key financial projections."

## 2. Company Description

*   **Prompt:** "Provide a detailed company description for an e-commerce startup named '[Company Name]'. Describe the business model, legal structure, and the company's history (or founding story). Detail the products or services offered and what makes them unique."

## 3. Market Analysis

*   **Prompt:** "Conduct a market analysis for the e-commerce industry, focusing on the [specific niche, e.g., 'sustainable fashion', 'gourmet pet food'] market. Identify the target audience, market size, and growth trends. Include an analysis of key competitors, their strengths, and weaknesses."

## 4. Organization and Management

*   **Prompt:** "Outline the organizational and management structure for '[Company Name]'. Detail the key management team members, their roles, responsibilities, and relevant experience. Include an organizational chart."

## 5. Products and Services

*   **Prompt:** "Create a detailed description of the products/services offered by '[Company Name]'. For each product, describe its features, benefits, and the value it provides to customers. Include information about the product sourcing, manufacturing process, and any intellectual property."

## 6. Marketing and Sales Strategy

*   **Prompt:** "Develop a comprehensive marketing and sales strategy for '[Company Name]'. This should include:
    *   **Online Marketing:** SEO, content marketing, social media marketing (specify platforms), email marketing, and paid advertising strategies.
    *   **Sales Strategy:** The sales process, from customer acquisition to conversion and retention.
    *   **Branding:** The brand's messaging, voice, and visual identity."

## 7. Financial Projections

*   **Prompt:** "Generate a 5-year financial projection for '[Company Name]'. This should include:
    *   **Startup Costs:** A breakdown of all initial expenses.
    *   **Sales Forecast:** A projection of sales revenue.
    *   **Profit and Loss Statement:** A projected P&L statement.
    *   **Cash Flow Statement:** A projected cash flow statement.
    *   **Balance Sheet:** A projected balance sheet.
    *   **Break-Even Analysis:** An analysis of the break-even point."

## 8. Funding Request (If Applicable)

*   **Prompt:** "Write a funding request for '[Company Name]'. Specify the amount of funding required, how the funds will be used, and the proposed terms for investors. This should be tailored for [e.g., 'venture capitalists', 'angel investors', 'a small business loan']."
