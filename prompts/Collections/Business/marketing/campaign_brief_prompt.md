# Integrated Campaign Brief (Based on ANA and IPA)

**Prompt:**
"Draft an integrated campaign brief for [Product] aligned with ANA and IPA best practices.
Include:
1. Objectives, KPIs, and success criteria.
2. Audience insights and journey stages.
3. Core message and creative territories.
4. Channel mix with budget allocation.
5. Measurement plan (MMM, attribution, lift).

Deliver a concise brief plus timeline."
