# Financial Modeling Blueprint (Based on CFA Institute practices)

**Prompt:**
"Create a financial model outline for [Business/Project] aligned with CFA Institute modeling best practices. Include:
1. Model structure (assumptions, drivers, outputs, checks).
2. Revenue build with cohort or pipeline logic.
3. Cost structure by fixed vs variable, and unit economics.
4. Cash flow, balance sheet, and working capital assumptions.
5. Scenario analysis (base/bull/bear) with key sensitivities.

Provide a model audit checklist and the top 10 model risks."
