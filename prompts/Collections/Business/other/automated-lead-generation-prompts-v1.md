# AI Agent Prompts for Automated Lead Generation

This series of prompts guides an AI agent in automating the process of lead generation by scraping websites and social media for contact information.

**Disclaimer:** Ensure that all lead generation activities comply with relevant privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) and the terms of service of the websites and social media platforms being scraped.

## 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Definition

**Prompt:**
"Define the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) for [Product/Service]. The ICP should include:
- Industry/Niche
- Company Size (revenue, number of employees)
- Job Titles of Key Decision-Makers
- Geographic Location
- Technologies Used (e.g., specific software or platforms)"

## 2. Source Identification

**Prompt:**
"Based on the ICP, identify the top 5 online sources for finding potential leads. These sources could include:
- Professional networking sites (e.g., LinkedIn)
- Industry-specific forums and online communities
- Business directories (e.g., Clutch, G2)
- Social media platforms (e.g., Twitter, Facebook Groups)
- Company websites and blogs"

## 3. Data Extraction Parameters

**Prompt:**
"For each identified source, specify the exact data points to be extracted for each lead. The standard data points should include:
- Full Name
- Job Title
- Company Name
- Company Website
- Email Address
- LinkedIn Profile URL
- Source of the lead"

## 4. Scraping and Data Collection

**Prompt:**
"Develop a scraping plan to collect the specified data from the identified sources. The plan should outline the scraping methodology for each source, including the use of any specific tools or libraries. For social media, specify how to search for relevant profiles and extract the required information. For websites, detail the process of finding contact pages or employee directories."

## 5. Data Cleaning and Formatting

**Prompt:**
"After collecting the raw data, create a process for cleaning and formatting it. This should include:
- Removing duplicate entries.
- Standardizing job titles (e.g., 'SVP' to 'Senior Vice President').
- Verifying email address formats.
- Formatting the data into a CSV file with clearly labeled columns corresponding to the data extraction parameters."

## 6. Ethical Considerations and Compliance Check

**Prompt:**
"Before initiating the scraping process, provide a checklist to ensure compliance with ethical guidelines and legal regulations. The checklist should include:
- Reviewing the Terms of Service for each source website/platform.
- Ensuring compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection laws.
- Implementing measures to avoid overloading website servers (e.g., rate limiting).
- A clear plan for the responsible and ethical use of the collected data."
