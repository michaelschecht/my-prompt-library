# Investor-Ready Pitch Narrative

## Objective
Turn **[BUSINESS SUMMARY]** into a compelling investor narrative and pitch structure.

## Inputs
- Business summary: [DESCRIPTION]
- Stage and traction: [METRICS OR PROXIES]
- Capital ask range: [$X]
- Target investor type: [ANGEL / SEED / VC]

## Analysis Instructions
Develop:
- Problem/solution and market opportunity storyline
- Why-now timing argument
- Traction evidence and proof points
- Scalable business model logic
- Team execution credibility
- Funding use plan and milestone path

## Required Output
1. 10-slide pitch storyline outline
2. Investor-priority metrics to highlight
3. Likely objections and responses
4. Funding ask and use-of-funds narrative
5. Closing investment thesis
