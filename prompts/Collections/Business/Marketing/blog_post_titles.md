# Blog Post Title Generation Prompts

This library provides a collection of prompts for generating creative and engaging blog post titles for various styles of content.

---

### 1. The Listicle

**Prompt:**
"Generate 10 compelling listicle titles for a blog post about `[topic]`. The titles should be catchy, promise a definitive guide, and be optimized for search engines."

**Example:**
"Generate 10 compelling listicle titles for a blog post about 'remote work productivity'. The titles should be catchy, promise a definitive guide, and be optimized for search engines."

---

### 2. The "How-To" Guide

**Prompt:**
"Create 5 'how-to' blog post titles that solve a common problem related to `[topic]`. The titles should be clear, concise, and action-oriented."

**Example:**
"Create 5 'how-to' blog post titles that solve a common problem related to 'starting a vegetable garden'. The titles should be clear, concise, and action-oriented."

---

### 3. The Controversial Take

**Prompt:**
"Write 3 blog post titles that present a controversial or contrarian viewpoint on `[topic]`. The titles should be designed to spark debate and engagement."

**Example:**
"Write 3 blog post titles that present a controversial or contrarian viewpoint on 'the 9-to-5 workday'. The titles should be designed to spark debate and engagement."

---

### 4. The "Secret of" or "Future of"

**Prompt:**
"Generate 5 blog post titles that reveal a 'secret' or predict the 'future' of `[topic]`. The titles should create a sense of intrigue and exclusivity."

**Example:**
"Generate 5 blog post titles that reveal a 'secret' or predict the 'future' of 'artificial intelligence in marketing'. The titles should create a sense of intrigue and exclusivity."

---

### 5. The Data-Driven Headline

**Prompt:**
"Craft 3 blog post titles that incorporate a specific statistic or data point about `[topic]`. The titles should be impactful and credible."

**Example:**
"Craft 3 blog post titles that incorporate the statistic '85% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations' for a blog post about 'the importance of online reviews'."

---

### 6. The Question-Based Title

**Prompt:**
"Write 5 engaging blog post titles in the form of a question about `[topic]`. The titles should make the reader curious to find the answer."

**Example:**
"Write 5 engaging blog post titles in the form of a question about 'the benefits of mindfulness meditation'. The titles should make the reader curious to find the answer."

---

### 7. The "Beginner's Guide"

**Prompt:**
"Create 3 welcoming and comprehensive blog post titles for a 'beginner's guide' to `[topic]`."

**Example:**
"Create 3 welcoming and comprehensive blog post titles for a 'beginner's guide' to 'learning to code'."

---

### 8. The "Mistakes to Avoid" Title

**Prompt:**
"Generate 5 titles for a blog post about common mistakes people make when `[doing something related to topic]`. The titles should be cautionary and helpful."

**Example:**
"Generate 5 titles for a blog post about common mistakes people make when 'investing in cryptocurrency'. The titles should be cautionary and helpful."
