# E-commerce Platform Integration Promo

## Purpose
Create an email marketing campaign to announce a new integration or partnership between two B2B software platforms (e.g., an e-commerce platform and a shipping software, or a CRM and a marketing automation tool) to drive joint adoption.

## Instructions
Act as a Partner Marketing Manager. Write a 2-part email campaign announcing a new, native integration between your software and another popular platform used by your target audience. The campaign should clearly explain the "better together" value proposition, how the integration saves time or money, and provide a clear path to activation.

Specify your platform (Platform A), the partner platform (Platform B), the primary benefit of the integration (e.g., automated data syncing, streamlined fulfillment), and the target audience (e.g., online store owners, sales teams).

## Output Format
Outline the integration promotional campaign as follows:
1. **Partnership Overview**: Define Platform A, Platform B, and the "Better Together" value proposition.
2. **Email 1: The Announcement (The "Why")**: The main launch email focusing on the pain point solved by connecting the two tools.
3. **Email 2: The Technical How-To / Case Study (The "How")**: A follow-up email providing a quick tutorial on setting up the integration or a brief story of a beta user who succeeded with it.

## Example
**Input:**
Platform A (Your Company): E-commerce Store Builder (e.g., Shopify alternative).
Platform B (Partner): Global Shipping and Fulfillment Software.
Benefit: Automatically sync orders to the shipping dashboard and instantly print discounted international labels.
Audience: E-commerce merchants processing 500+ orders/month.

**Output:**
### 1. Partnership Overview
**Platforms:** [Your E-commerce Builder] + [Shipping Partner]
**Better Together:** Stop manually exporting CSVs. Sync orders instantly and access discounted shipping rates in one click.

### Email 1: The Announcement
**Subject:** BIG NEWS: [Your Platform] + [Shipping Partner] are officially integrated 🤝
**Body:**
Hi [Name],
Fulfilling orders shouldn't take longer than making the sales. If you've been manually exporting spreadsheets to get your shipping labels printed, we have great news.

Today, we are thrilled to announce our native integration with **[Shipping Partner]**.

By connecting your store, you can now:
- **Auto-sync orders:** Every new order instantly appears in your [Shipping Partner] dashboard.
- **Save on shipping:** Access pre-negotiated, discounted rates for USPS, UPS, and FedEx.
- **Automate tracking:** Tracking numbers are automatically sent back to your customers.

Ready to streamline your fulfillment?

[Button: Connect [Shipping Partner] Now]

Cheers,
The [Platform A] Team

*(Continues for Email 2)*
