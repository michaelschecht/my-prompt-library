# LLM Prompt Sequence for Social Media Content Automation

This sequence of prompts guides an LLM to create a content calendar and generate engaging social media posts for a small business.

## 1. Business & Audience Definition

**Prompt:**
"Describe the small business for which we are creating social media content. Please include:
- **Business Name:**
- **Industry:**
- **Products/Services:**
- **Target Audience:** (demographics, interests, pain points)
- **Brand Voice/Tone:** (e.g., professional, friendly, witty, inspirational)
- **Social Media Platforms:** (e.g., Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)"

## 2. Content Pillar Identification

**Prompt:**
"Based on the business and audience profile, identify 3-5 core content pillars (key themes or topics) that will be the foundation of the social media strategy. For each pillar, provide a brief explanation of why it is relevant to the target audience."

## 3. Content Calendar Generation

**Prompt:**
"Generate a one-week social media content calendar based on the defined content pillars. The calendar should be in a table format with the following columns:
- **Day of the Week:**
- **Platform:**
- **Content Pillar:**
- **Post Type:** (e.g., Image Post, Video, Story, Blog Post Link)
- **Draft Headline/Hook:**"

## 4. Post Copywriting

**Prompt:**
"For each entry in the content calendar, write the full social media post copy. The copy should:
- Be tailored to the specific platform.
- Align with the brand voice and tone.
- Include a clear call-to-action (CTA).
- Use relevant hashtags.
- Be engaging and encourage interaction."

## 5. Visual Content Suggestion

**Prompt:**
"For each post in the content calendar, suggest ideas for the accompanying visual content. The suggestions should be specific and creative. For example:
- 'A short, time-lapse video of the product being made.'
- 'A high-quality photo of a customer using the product in a real-life scenario.'
- 'An infographic that visualizes a key statistic related to the industry.'"

## 6. Performance Review & Iteration

**Prompt:**
"After one week of posting, analyze the performance of the social media content based on the following (hypothetical) metrics:
- **Reach:**
- **Engagement Rate:** (likes, comments, shares)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** (for posts with links)

Based on this analysis, identify which content pillars and post types are performing best. Suggest adjustments to the content strategy for the following week to optimize for better results."
