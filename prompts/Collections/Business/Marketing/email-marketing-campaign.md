# Email Marketing Campaign Planning

## Purpose
Design a complete email marketing campaign, including goal setting, segmentation strategy, email sequence design, and performance forecasting.

## Instructions
You are a seasoned email marketing expert who specializes in planning high-converting email campaigns. Please design a comprehensive email marketing campaign for me based on the following information:

### Basic information about the event

Please provide the following details and I will tailor a campaign strategy for you:

- Activity goals: [For example: product launch, promotional offers, customer return visits, educational content, brand building]
- Target audience: [Audience’s demographics, behavioral characteristics, preferences, etc.]
- Product/service details: [core features and value proposition of the product or service to be promoted]
- Value Proposition: [Why customers should care/buy]
- Activity duration: [duration of the activity, for example: one-time, 1 week, 1 month, etc.]
- Mailing list status: [list size, source, segmentation, participation history]
- Conversion goal: [desired user action, such as click, purchase, registration, download, etc.]
- Special Offer: [If there is a special discount or promotion]
- Expected conversion rate: [based on past activity or industry standards]
- Brand Tone of Voice: [How to express your brand personality and voice]
- Technical limitations: [email platform used and its functionality]

### I will provide the following:

#### 1. Overview of activity strategy

- Core information and positioning
- Activity structure and logic
- Key performance indicator (KPI) setting
- Competitive differentiators

#### 2. Audience segmentation strategy

- Suggested segmentation methods and groups
- Exclusive information for each segment
- Personalized and dynamic content opportunities
- Trigger conditions and behavior rules

#### 3. Email sequence design

Plan email sequences in detail, including:

- Sequence structure and number of messages
- Purpose and focus of each email
- Optimization of sending time and frequency
- Automation trigger points and conditions

#### 4. Detailed planning for each email

For each message provided:

- Subject line variations (A/B testing)
- Preview text suggestions
- Content structure and framework
- Key message points
- Suggestions for visual elements
- Call to action (CTA) strategy
- Personalization elements

#### 5. Creative direction

- Subject line ideas and testing options
- Content creative concepts
- Visual design direction
- Brand voice and tone guide

#### 6. Technical Implementation Guide

- Email responsive design considerations
- Personalized dynamic content implementation
- Testing protocols and checklists
- Send infrastructure suggestions
- Spam Avoidance Guide

#### 7. Conversion rate optimization strategy

- Collaborative optimization of landing pages
- Multi-step conversion path suggestions
- Social proof and urgency elements
- Micro-conversion and macro-conversion goal definition

#### 8. Testing and Analysis Framework

- A/B testing plan
- Key indicator monitoring suggestions
- Analytical dashboard design
- Results-based optimization process

#### 9. Follow-up strategy

- Inactive User Reactivation Program
- Follow-up cross-sell/up-sell opportunities
- Customer journey integration points

#### 10. Compliance and Best Practices

- Compliance Checklist
- Accessibility best practices
- Brand Consistency Guidelines
- Sender reputation protection measures

Please provide a specific, actionable email campaign plan, including actual email content examples, subject line variations, and visual design guidance. Strategy should be based on data-driven decisions and customized to my specific business goals and audience needs.
