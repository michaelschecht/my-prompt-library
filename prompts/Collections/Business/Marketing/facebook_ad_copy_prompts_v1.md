# LLM Prompt Series for Facebook Ad Copy Generation

This series of prompts guides an LLM in generating high-converting ad copy for a Facebook marketing campaign.

## 1. Audience Persona Definition

**Prompt:**
"Create a detailed audience persona for a Facebook ad campaign selling [Product/Service]. The persona should include:
- Demographics (age, gender, location, income level, education)
- Psychographics (interests, hobbies, values, lifestyle)
- Pain Points (specific problems or challenges they face)
- Goals & Aspirations (what they want to achieve)
- Online Behavior (social media usage, preferred content formats)"

## 2. Core Messaging & Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

**Prompt:**
"Based on the persona for [Product/Service], define the core message and Unique Selling Proposition (USP). What is the single most important thing the audience should know? What makes this product/service different from and better than the competition? The USP should be clear, concise, and compelling."

## 3. Hook & Headline Generation

**Prompt:**
"Generate 5 attention-grabbing headlines for a Facebook ad targeting the [Persona Name] persona. The headlines should be short, punchy, and create curiosity or highlight a key benefit. Each headline should be a maximum of 10 words."

## 4. Ad Body Copy Creation

**Prompt:**
"Write 3 variations of the main ad body copy. The copy should:
- Immediately hook the reader with the persona's main pain point or desire.
- Clearly present the [Product/Service] as the solution.
- Highlight 2-3 key benefits (not just features).
- Use a tone that resonates with the [Persona Name] persona (e.g., professional, friendly, humorous).
- Be easy to read and scan, using short paragraphs and bullet points."

## 5. Call-to-Action (CTA) Crafting

**Prompt:**
"Generate 5 clear and compelling Call-to-Action (CTA) options for the ad. The CTAs should be action-oriented and create a sense of urgency or exclusivity. Examples: 'Shop Now & Get 20% Off', 'Download Your Free Guide', 'Join the Waitlist'."

## 6. A/B Testing Variations

**Prompt:**
"Create a set of A/B testing variations for the ad campaign. Provide 3 alternative versions for each of the following components:
- Headline
- First line of the ad body
- CTA
This will result in a total of 9 variations to test."
