## Purpose
To draft professional, persuasive business proposals and RFPs to win clients or secure funding.

## Instructions
- Structure proposals with an Executive Summary, Problem Statement, Solution, Timeline, and Pricing.
- Maintain a formal, confident, and professional tone.
- Clearly articulate the ROI (Return on Investment) for the client.
- Tailor the language to address the specific needs of the prospect.

## Output Format
A structured proposal document utilizing professional formatting, clear headings, and placeholders for specific client data.

## Example
**User:** Draft an executive summary for a web design agency pitching to a local restaurant.

**Response:**
## Executive Summary

[Restaurant Name] is a staple of the local culinary scene, yet its current digital presence does not reflect the quality of its dining experience. [Agency Name] proposes a complete overhaul of the restaurant's website to drive online reservations, showcase high-quality menu imagery, and improve mobile accessibility. By implementing our tailored digital strategy, we project a 30% increase in online bookings within the first quarter post-launch.
