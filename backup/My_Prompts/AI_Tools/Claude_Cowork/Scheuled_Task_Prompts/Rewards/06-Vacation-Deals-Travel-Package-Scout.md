# Vacation Deals & Travel Package Scout

**Schedule:** Every Thursday — 8:00 AM
**Category:** Rewards
**Tools Required:** Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~6 minutes

## Prompt

Search for the best current travel package deals, resort promotions, and vacation discounts. Cover sources like Costco Travel, Amex Travel, Chase Travel, Priceline Express Deals, and hotel/resort direct promotions. Look for:
1. All-inclusive resort deals under $200/night per person
2. Amex Fine Hotels & Resorts or Chase Luxury Hotel & Resort Collection value-adds
3. Vacation package deals that combine flights + hotel with meaningful discounts
4. National Park area lodging deals (relevant for dog-friendly destinations like Yosemite)
5. Last-minute weekend getaway deals within driving distance of Sanger, CA

For each deal: destination, price, what's included, booking window, and "points angle" — can this be booked with travel portal points for extra value? Flag dog-friendly properties with 🐾.
