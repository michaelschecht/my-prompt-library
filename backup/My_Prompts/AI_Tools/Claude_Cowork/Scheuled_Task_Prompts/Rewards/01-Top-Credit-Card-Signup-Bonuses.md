# Top Credit Card Signup Bonuses

**Schedule:** Every Monday — 8:00 AM
**Category:** Rewards
**Tools Required:** Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~5 minutes

## Prompt

Search for the best credit card signup bonuses currently available this week. Check sources like The Points Guy, NerdWallet, Doctor of Credit, and CardRatings. Look for:
1. Cards with elevated limited-time offers above their standard bonus (e.g. 100k+ point offers)
2. New card launches with intro bonuses
3. Any referral bonuses that exceed public offers
4. Business card offers worth noting separately

For each top pick: card name, bonus amount, minimum spend requirement, time to earn, annual fee, and estimated cash/travel value of the bonus at standard redemption rates. Flag any offers expiring within 14 days. Rank the top 5 by value-to-effort ratio.
