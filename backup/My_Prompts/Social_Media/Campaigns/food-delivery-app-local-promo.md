# Food Delivery App Local Promo Campaign

## Purpose
Create a hyper-local social media promotional campaign to increase orders for a food delivery app within specific city limits or neighborhoods.

## Instructions
Act as a local marketing manager for a food delivery service. Develop a targeted 2-week Instagram and Facebook campaign aimed at boosting lunch and dinner orders in a newly launched city. The campaign should highlight partnerships with beloved local restaurants, offer aggressive time-sensitive discounts, and utilize location-based advertising.

Specify the target city/neighborhood, key local restaurant partners, and the primary promotional offer (e.g., 50% off first order, free delivery weekend).

## Output Format
Provide the local promo strategy in the following format:
1. **Local Campaign Overview**: The city, target demographics (e.g., college students, busy families), and the core promotional offer.
2. **Hyper-Local Targeting Strategy**: Detailed parameters for Facebook/Instagram Ads (e.g., radius targeting around specific restaurants or business districts).
3. **Content Mix & Visual Style**:
   - **User-Generated Content (UGC)**: How to encourage and leverage customer photos.
   - **Restaurant Partner Spotlights**: Highlighting local chefs and popular dishes.
4. **Daily Promotional Schedule (Weeks 1-2)**: Include key messaging for high-intent times (e.g., 11 AM lunch rush, 4 PM dinner prep).
5. **Influencer/Micro-Influencer Activation**: Brief for partnering with local food bloggers (e.g., under 10k followers) for authentic reviews.

## Example
**Input:**
City: Austin, Texas (specifically downtown and university areas).
Partners: Local BBQ joints and popular taco trucks.
Offer: Free delivery on all local partner orders for the first week, plus 20% off with a promo code.

**Output:**
### 1. Local Campaign Overview
**City:** Austin, TX (Downtown & UT Campus).
**Demographic:** Tech workers in offices, UT students.
**Offer:** "Keep Austin Eating: Free Delivery + 20% Off Local Favorites."

### 2. Hyper-Local Targeting
**Facebook/IG Ads:** 3-mile radius around partner restaurants. Targeting interests in "BBQ," "Tacos," "University of Texas," and "Foodie."

*(Continues with content mix and daily schedule)*
