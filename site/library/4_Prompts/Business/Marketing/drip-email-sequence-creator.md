# ✉️ Drip Email Sequence Creator

## Purpose
To create a high-converting, 5-day automated email sequence for welcoming new subscribers or users.

## Instructions
Replace the bracketed variables with your product details and target audience.

## Prompt
```text
Act as an expert email marketer. Write a 5-day welcome drip email sequence for a new subscriber to my [Newsletter/Product Name], which helps [Target Audience] achieve [Desired Outcome].

Follow this structure:
Email 1 (Immediate): Welcome, deliver the lead magnet/promise, and set expectations.
Email 2 (Day 2): A relatable story highlighting the problem the subscriber faces.
Email 3 (Day 3): The "Aha!" moment and introducing our unique solution.
Email 4 (Day 4): Social proof, testimonials, and addressing objections.
Email 5 (Day 5): A strong Call to Action (CTA) to purchase or upgrade.

For each email, provide:
- 2 Subject line options
- Body copy (keep it conversational and under 250 words)
- The core CTA
```

## Output Format
Markdown document separating each of the 5 emails clearly with subject lines and body copy.

## Example Output
**Email 1**
Subject 1: Welcome to the club! Here is your guide.
Body: Hi [Name], I'm thrilled to have you...

## Tips for Use
Ensure the tone matches your brand's voice. Use placeholder tags like `{{first_name}}` for easy import into your email marketing software.
