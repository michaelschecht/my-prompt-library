# B2B Lead Gen Campaign

## Purpose
This prompt generates a comprehensive, multi-touch B2B email sequence designed to capture and nurture high-value leads. It focuses on addressing specific business pain points and offering clear value propositions.

## Instructions
I want you to act as an expert B2B Demand Generation Marketer. Your task is to write a 4-email outbound sequence targeting [Target Job Title, e.g., VP of Engineering].

The goal of this sequence is to get them to book a 15-minute discovery call regarding our product, [Product Name], which helps them [Core Value Proposition].

Please structure the sequence as follows:
- **Email 1 (Day 1):** The Hook. Highly personalized, referencing a common industry pain point and briefly introducing our solution.
- **Email 2 (Day 4):** The Value Add. Provide a piece of actionable advice or link to a relevant case study/resource without asking for a meeting.
- **Email 3 (Day 9):** The Ask. Clear explanation of ROI and a direct, low-friction Call to Action to book a call.
- **Email 4 (Day 14):** The Breakup. A polite closing email leaving the door open for future contact.

For each email, provide two Subject Line options. Keep the tone professional, concise, and focused on the recipient's challenges.

## Output Format
Markdown format with clear headings for each Email in the sequence, including Day sent, Subject Lines, and Body Copy.

## Example
**Input Parameters:**
- Target: VP of Engineering
- Product Name: CodeSecure
- Value Proposition: Automate compliance audits and reduce code vulnerability scan times by 50% without slowing down CI/CD pipelines.

## Tips for Use
- Replace the bracketed variables with your specific product details before running the prompt.
- The shorter the email body, the higher the conversion rate in B2B outreach. If the AI generates overly long emails, prompt it to "cut the length of each email by 30%".
