# Email Marketing Drip

A prompt to create an engaging automated email drip campaign to nurture leads and convert them into customers.


## Purpose
A prompt to create an engaging automated email drip campaign to nurture leads and convert them into customers.

## Instructions
Act as a seasoned email marketing copywriter. I need a [Number]-part email drip campaign for users who have just downloaded my lead magnet: [Name/Topic of Lead Magnet]. The ultimate goal of this sequence is to sell my core offer: [Core Offer Name and Description].

Please write the complete sequence. For each email, include:
1. A compelling subject line.
2. Preview text.
3. The email body copy.
4. A clear, singular Call to Action (CTA).

Sequence structure:
- Email 1: Delivery of the lead magnet & welcome.
- Email 2: High-value educational content related to the topic.
- Email 3: A case study or testimonial highlighting the core offer.
- Email 4: Soft pitch for the core offer.
- Email 5: Direct pitch with urgency/scarcity (if applicable).

## Output Format
Markdown with distinct sections for each email in the sequence.

## Example
User input: 5-part campaign. Lead magnet: "Top 10 SEO Tips". Core offer: "SEO Masterclass Course".

Output: [Full sequence of 5 detailed emails designed to nurture and sell]

## Tips for Use
Define the tone clearly (e.g., conversational, authoritative) to ensure it matches your brand voice.
