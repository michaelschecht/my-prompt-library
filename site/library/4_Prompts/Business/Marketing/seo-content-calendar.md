# SEO Content Calendar

A prompt to generate a strategic SEO-focused content calendar based on a specific niche and target keywords.


## Purpose
A prompt to generate a strategic SEO-focused content calendar based on a specific niche and target keywords.

## Instructions
Act as an expert SEO strategist. My website focuses on the niche of [Niche/Industry]. My primary target audience is [Target Audience]. My main goal is to [Goal, e.g., drive organic traffic, generate leads].

Please generate a comprehensive 1-month SEO content calendar. I want to publish [Number] blog posts per week.

For each blog post, provide:
1. A catchy, SEO-optimized title.
2. The primary target keyword.
3. 2-3 secondary target keywords.
4. A brief outline or summary of what the article should cover (3-4 bullet points).
5. The target search intent (e.g., Informational, Transactional).

## Output Format
Markdown table or a structured list.

## Example
User input: Niche: Vegan fitness. Goal: drive traffic. 2 posts per week.

Output: [Detailed 4-week content calendar with optimized titles, keywords, and outlines]

## Tips for Use
Mention any specific seasonal trends or upcoming events in your niche for more relevant topics.
