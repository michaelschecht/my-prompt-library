# 📝 SEO Blog Post Outline Builder

## Purpose
To create a structurally sound, SEO-optimized outline for a blog post targeting specific keywords, ensuring comprehensiveness and high search ranking potential.

## Instructions
Provide the primary keyword and any secondary keywords you wish to target.

## Prompt
```text
Act as an expert SEO content strategist. I want to write a comprehensive, ranking-focused blog post targeting the primary keyword: "[Insert Primary Keyword]".
Secondary keywords to include: "[Insert Secondary Keywords]".

Please generate a detailed blog post outline. The outline must include:
1. An engaging H1 Title (provide 3 options).
2. An introduction section detailing the hook and the problem being solved.
3. H2 and H3 subheadings logically structured to cover the topic comprehensively. Incorporate the secondary keywords naturally into these headings where appropriate.
4. For each H2/H3 section, provide 2-3 bullet points describing what the content should cover.
5. Suggestions for places to include internal or external links, statistics, or charts.
6. A conclusion with a clear Call to Action (CTA).
```

## Output Format
Hierarchical Markdown outline using H1, H2, and H3 tags.

## Example Output
### H1: The Ultimate Guide to SEO in 2024
#### H2: Why SEO Matters
- Explain organic vs paid traffic.
- [Insert Statistic on search volume].

## Tips for Use
Run a quick Google search on your primary keyword and ensure your outline covers topics the top 3 ranking articles cover, plus adds unique value.
