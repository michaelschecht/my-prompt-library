# Product Launch Sequence

A comprehensive prompt to generate a highly effective email and social media sequence for a new product launch.


## Purpose
A comprehensive prompt to generate a highly effective email and social media sequence for a new product launch.

## Instructions
Act as an expert digital marketing strategist. I am launching a new product called [Product Name]. It solves the problem of [Problem] by [Solution]. The target audience is [Audience].

Please create a complete launch sequence including:
1. A pre-launch teaser email (sent 1 week before launch).
2. The official launch announcement email.
3. A follow-up email focusing on a key feature or case study (sent 2 days after launch).
4. A "last chance/urgency" email (sent 24 hours before a special launch discount expires).
5. Three engaging social media post captions tailored for [Platform].

Ensure the tone is [Tone, e.g., exciting, professional, persuasive].

## Output Format
Markdown with clear headings for each email and social media post.

## Example
User input: Launching "TaskMaster", an AI task manager. Target audience: busy professionals. Tone: encouraging and professional.

Output: [Detailed email templates and social media posts tailored to TaskMaster]

## Tips for Use
Provide as much detail as possible about your product's unique value proposition and target audience.
