# 📱 Viral Social Media Hook Generator

## Purpose
To generate attention-grabbing opening lines (hooks) for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or Twitter threads to maximize engagement and watch time.

## Instructions
Copy and paste the prompt below into your LLM. Replace the bracketed information with your specific product or topic.

## Prompt
```text
Act as an expert social media copywriter who specializes in viral, short-form content. I am creating content about [Insert Topic/Product].

Please generate 10 high-converting hooks. Use the following psychological frameworks:
1. The Negative Hook (e.g., "Stop doing X if you want Y")
2. The Curiosity Gap (e.g., "The secret to X that nobody tells you")
3. The Authority Hook (e.g., "I analyzed 100 X, here is what I found")
4. The Relatable Hook (e.g., "Does anyone else struggle with X?")

Keep the hooks under 15 words. Ensure they immediately capture attention within the first 3 seconds of a video or the first line of text.
```

## Output Format
A numbered list of 10 hooks categorized by the psychological framework used.

## Example Output
1. The Negative Hook: Stop posting on TikTok at 9 AM if you want to go viral.
2. The Curiosity Gap: The secret ChatGPT prompt that saves me 10 hours a week.

## Tips for Use
Combine these hooks with fast-paced editing and strong visuals for maximum effect.
