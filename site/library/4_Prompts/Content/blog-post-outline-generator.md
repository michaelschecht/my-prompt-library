# Blog Post Outline Generator

## Purpose
To generate a structured and SEO-friendly outline for a blog post based on a given topic.

## Instructions
Please create a comprehensive blog post outline for the following topic:
- **Topic:** [Insert Topic]
- **Target Audience:** [Insert Target Audience]
- **Primary Keyword:** [Insert Primary Keyword]

The outline should include:
1. A catchy working title.
2. An introduction section (hook, context, thesis/purpose).
3. 3-5 main body sections with descriptive H2 headings.
4. 2-3 sub-points (H3 headings) under each main section.
5. A conclusion section summarizing key takeaways and a Call to Action (CTA).

## Output Format
A structured Markdown list representing the outline.

## Example
**Input:**
- Topic: Benefits of remote work for companies
- Target Audience: HR managers and executives
- Primary Keyword: remote work benefits

**Output:**
**Working Title:** Top 5 Remote Work Benefits: Why Your Company Should Embrace the Shift

1. **Introduction**
   - Hook: The modern workplace has evolved beyond the office walls.
   - Context: The rapid adoption of distributed teams post-2020.
   - Thesis: Embracing remote work benefits not only employees but drives significant advantages for the company's bottom line and culture.

2. **H2: Significant Cost Savings**
   - H3: Reduced real estate and overhead expenses.
   - H3: Lower operational costs (utilities, supplies).

3. **H2: Access to a Global Talent Pool**
   - H3: Breaking geographical barriers for hiring.
   - H3: Increasing diversity and varied perspectives.

4. **H2: Boosted Employee Productivity and Retention**
   - H3: Fewer office distractions and customized work environments.
   - H3: Higher job satisfaction leading to lower turnover rates.

5. **Conclusion**
   - Summary of the main benefits.
   - CTA: Evaluate your current policies and consider transitioning to a hybrid or fully remote model today.

## Tips for Use
- Be clear about the target audience so the outline's tone and structure align appropriately.
- Ensure the primary keyword is naturally incorporated into the suggested H2 headings.
