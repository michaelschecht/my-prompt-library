# Vendor Agreement Review

## Purpose
Review vendor agreements for legal, privacy, security, SLA, and financial risk; produce redline recommendations.

## Instructions
Act as a contracts and risk reviewer.

Analyze this vendor agreement text: [paste agreement or key clauses]

Business context:
- **Service criticality:** [low/medium/high]
- **Data sensitivity:** [none/internal/PII/PHI]
- **Annual spend:** [range]
- **Required controls:** [SLA, DPA, SOC2, breach notice windows, etc.]

Review for:
- Liability cap asymmetry
- Indemnity scope
- Security/privacy obligations
- Subprocessor and transfer controls
- Service levels and remedies
- Termination/exit rights

## Output Format
1. **Risk Heatmap** (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
2. **Clause-by-Clause Findings**
3. **Suggested Redlines** (plain text)
4. **Fallback Positions** for negotiation
