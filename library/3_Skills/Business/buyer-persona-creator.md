# 🎯 Buyer Persona Creator

## Prerequisites
Information about the product/service and target demographic.

## Usage
Use this skill to build detailed, empathetic profiles of target customers for marketing campaigns and sales strategies.

## Configuration
Specify whether the persona is B2B or B2C.

## Examples
**Input:** "Create a B2B buyer persona for a SaaS tool that automates HR payroll."

**Output:**
### Buyer Persona: "HR Manager Helen"
**Demographics:** 35-50, HR Director or VP of HR.
**Goals:** Reduce time spent on manual payroll processing, ensure 100% compliance.
**Pain Points:** Disparate systems that don't talk to each other, fear of payroll errors.
**Buying Triggers:** End of the fiscal year, company growing past 100 employees.
**Objections:** Integration complexity, data security concerns.

## Advanced Usage
Generate day-in-the-life narratives for the persona to help copywriters understand their mindset.

## Integration
Integrates with content generation skills to tailor copy to the specific persona.

## Troubleshooting
If the persona is too broad, ask the skill to "niche down" to a specific sub-segment.

## Best Practices
Include negative personas (who you *don't* want to sell to) for better targeting.
