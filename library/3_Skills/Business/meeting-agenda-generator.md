# 🗓️ Meeting Agenda Generator

## Prerequisites
Context about the meeting's purpose, attendees, and desired outcomes.

## Usage
Use this skill to quickly structure highly effective, time-boxed meeting agendas that prevent tangents and ensure clear action items are derived.

## Configuration
Specify the total duration of the meeting.

## Examples
**Input:** "Create an agenda for a 45-minute sprint planning meeting with the engineering team. Goal: finalize tasks for the next 2 weeks."

**Output:**
### Sprint Planning Meeting (45 Minutes)
**Objective:** Finalize tasks for the upcoming 2-week sprint.

**Agenda:**
1. **[00:00 - 00:05] Check-in & Icebreaker:** Quick mood check.
2. **[00:05 - 00:15] Review Previous Sprint:** What went well? What didn't?
3. **[00:15 - 00:35] Backlog Grooming & Task Estimation:** Assign points to top priority items.
4. **[00:35 - 00:40] Finalize Sprint Goal:** Ensure everyone agrees on the primary objective.
5. **[00:40 - 00:45] Action Items & Wrap-up:** Who is doing what next?

## Advanced Usage
Ask the skill to generate specific questions for the meeting facilitator to ask during each agenda block.

## Integration
Integrates with calendar agents to automatically append agendas to calendar invites.

## Troubleshooting
If the agenda blocks exceed the total meeting time, explicitly instruct the model to "ensure the sum of all time blocks equals exactly [duration]".

## Best Practices
Always include a buffer for Q&A and explicitly define the desired outcome at the top.

## Performance Considerations
Lightweight; requires standard LLM reasoning capabilities.
