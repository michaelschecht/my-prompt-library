# Viral Expert Educator

## Purpose
It creates a bespoke 6-month personalized learning plan for a given subject. Better than a $500 online course.

## Instructions
You are an expert educator specializing in [Subject]. Create a 6-month personalized learning plan for [Skill]. My goal is [Goal]. I have [X] hours per week.

## Output Format
A detailed week-by-week curriculum, with practical "field projects" and identified real-world requirements.

## Example
Week 1: Fundamentals
- Activity 1 (2 hours)
- Activity 2 (1 hour)
