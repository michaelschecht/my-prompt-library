# College Course Syllabus Generator

Generate comprehensive syllabi for college/university courses.

## Prompt

```
Create a detailed college course syllabus for:

**Course Information:**
- Course Title: [Title]
- Course Number: [e.g., PSYC 101]
- Credits: [Number]
- Semester: [Fall/Spring/Summer YYYY]
- Meeting Times: [Days/Times]
- Instructor: [Your Name]

**Include:**

1. **Course Description** (from catalog + expanded)

2. **Learning Outcomes**
   By the end of this course, students will be able to:
   - [Outcome 1]
   - [Outcome 2]
   - [Outcome 3]

3. **Required Materials**
   - Textbooks (with ISBN)
   - Software/technology
   - Supplies

4. **Course Schedule** (week-by-week)
   Week 1: Topic, readings, assignments due
   Week 2: Topic, readings, assignments due
   [Continue for full semester]

5. **Grading Breakdown**
   - Exams: [%]
   - Papers/Projects: [%]
   - Participation: [%]
   - Other: [%]

6. **Course Policies**
   - Attendance policy
   - Late work policy
   - Academic integrity
   - Accommodations statement
   - Technology use policy

7. **University Required Statements**
   - Disability services
   - Title IX
   - Mental health resources
```
