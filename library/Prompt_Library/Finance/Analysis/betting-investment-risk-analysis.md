# Betting & Investment Risk Analysis Dataset

## Objective
Create a structured dataset that compares expected return (percentage gain or loss) across betting, gambling, and investment activities. The goal is to quantify player/investor edge versus house/market edge.

## Scope of Analysis

### 1. Sports Betting
- Single game (spread / over-under)
- 2-leg parlay
- 3-leg parlay
- 4-leg parlay
- 5-leg parlay
- Additional bet types (moneyline, same-game parlays, teasers, live betting)

### 2. Casino Gambling
- Blackjack (3:2 payout)
- Blackjack (6:5 payout)
- Roulette (American & European)
- Craps (pass line, don't pass, other bets)
- Keno
- Slot machines
- Additional casino games

### 3. Stock Market
- S&P 500 (1 month, 1 year, 5 years)
- Dow Jones (1 month, 1 year, 5 years)
- International ETFs (1 month, 1 year, 5 years)
- Additional asset classes (individual stocks, sector ETFs, index funds)

### 4. Bond Market
- Government bonds
- Corporate bonds
- Bond ETFs
- Variations by duration and credit quality

### 5. Derivatives & Advanced Investing
- Options (buying calls/puts, selling covered calls)
- Futures trading
- Leveraged ETFs
- Day trading
- Additional speculative instruments

### 6. Prediction Markets
- Real-world examples (elections, event contracts)
- Multiple examples with varying risk profiles

## Data Requirements
- Calculate expected return (%) for each item
- Display ONLY the percentage (no descriptions in final dataset)
- Examples:
  - -5.26%
  - +10%

## Output Format
- Markdown (.md)
- Group results by category
- Each entry contains only the percentage

## Additional Requirements
1. Expand dataset significantly beyond listed items
2. Ensure full spectrum from player advantage → house advantage
3. Prepare data for future visualization:
   - Left: player/investor edge (positive)
   - Right: house edge (negative)
