# Crypto Rotation & Dominance Report

## Objective
Assess crypto market rotation to identify where risk/reward is strongest right now.

## Inputs
- Universe: [BTC, ETH, ALTS]
- Timeframe: [1D / 1W / 1M]
- Risk profile: [CONSERVATIVE / BALANCED / AGGRESSIVE]

## Analysis Instructions
Analyze rotation across:
- BTC
- ETH
- Large-cap alts
- Mid/small-cap alts
- Stablecoin flow and liquidity context

Include:
- BTC dominance trend and implication
- Risk-on vs risk-off behavior inside crypto
- Segment relative strength/weakness
- Liquidity durability and momentum quality

## Required Output
1. Current rotation phase classification
2. Segment with best current risk/reward
3. Segment with highest avoid risk
4. Tactical positioning ideas
5. Signals that would confirm a rotation change
