# Viral Social Strategist

## Purpose
It replaces a full social media team and comes up with viral ideas for a topic.

## Instructions
Act as a viral strategist. Generate 5 post ideas about [Topic]. Include 3 scroll-stopping hooks, visual concepts, and A/B caption variations for each.

## Output Format
A detailed post idea with hooks, visual concepts, and caption variations.

## Example
Post Idea 1: Why cold pizza is holding your productivity back
Hook 1: Did you know cold pizza can save you 10 hours a week?
Hook 2: Stop eating hot pizza right now.
Hook 3: The secret to productivity is in your fridge.
Visual Concept: A split screen showing someone struggling to eat hot pizza while working, compared to someone efficiently eating cold pizza.
Caption A: Hot pizza is so 2023. Cold pizza is where it's at. #productivity #coldpizza
Caption B: Save time and eat cold pizza. #lifehack #pizza
