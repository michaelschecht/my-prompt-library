# EdTech Platform User Acquisition Campaign

## Purpose
Design a comprehensive social media marketing campaign to acquire new users for an educational technology platform.

## Instructions
Act as a growth marketer for an EdTech company. Create a detailed 4-week user acquisition campaign tailored for a specific platform (e.g., coding bootcamp, language learning app, K-12 tutoring service). The campaign should span across multiple channels like YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, focusing on value, student success stories, and compelling offers.

Define the target audience (e.g., parents, college students, career switchers), the platform's core offering, and the primary acquisition hook (e.g., free trial, discount code).

## Output Format
Structure the campaign plan as follows:
1. **Campaign Overview & Target Audience**: Define the ideal user persona, the core offering, and the acquisition goal.
2. **Channel Strategy & Tactics**:
   - **YouTube**: Long-form educational content and pre-roll ads.
   - **TikTok/Instagram Reels**: Short, engaging tips and student testimonials.
3. **Weekly Content & Ad Schedule (Weeks 1-4)**:
   - Week 1: Problem Awareness
   - Week 2: Solution Introduction
   - Week 3: Social Proof & Testimonials
   - Week 4: Urgency & Offer
4. **Acquisition Funnel & Retargeting**: Outline the steps from ad click to sign-up, including retargeting strategies for users who don't convert immediately.

## Example
**Input:**
Platform: Online UX/UI design bootcamp.
Audience: Career switchers aged 25-35.
Acquisition Hook: 7-day free trial with access to the first module.

**Output:**
### 1. Campaign Overview & Target Audience
**Objective:** Acquire 500 new free trial sign-ups with a 20% conversion rate to paid within 30 days.
**Audience:** Professionals looking to transition into tech without a degree, earning $50k-$80k currently.

### 2. Channel Strategy
- **YouTube:** "A Day in the Life of a UX Designer" video; ads targeted at users searching for "how to become a UI designer."
- **Instagram Reels:** Quick design tips, portfolio critiques, and success stories from alumni.

*(Continues with weekly schedule and funnel details)*
