# Market Research and Customer Validation

## Objective
Validate demand for **[BUSINESS IDEA]** and identify the highest-value customer segment.

## Inputs
- Idea summary: [DESCRIPTION]
- Target segment hypothesis: [ICP]
- Region: [GEOGRAPHY]
- Validation budget/timebox: [X DAYS / $Y]

## Analysis Instructions
Analyze:
- Ideal customer profiles and purchase triggers
- Top customer pain points and current alternatives
- Market segmentation and size assumptions
- Competitor map (direct, indirect, substitute)
- Validation experiments (interviews, landing tests, pilots, pre-sales)

## Required Output
1. Final ICP recommendation
2. Demand confidence score (Low/Medium/High)
3. Top objections and response strategy
4. 30-day validation plan
5. Go / iterate / pivot decision
