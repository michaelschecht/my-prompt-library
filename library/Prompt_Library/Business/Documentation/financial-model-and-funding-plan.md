# Financial Model and Funding Plan

## Objective
Build a practical 3-year financial and funding plan for **[BUSINESS NAME]**.

## Inputs
- Stage: [IDEA / MVP / EARLY REVENUE]
- Revenue model: [SUBSCRIPTION / TRANSACTION / SERVICES / HYBRID]
- Team size assumptions: [HEADCOUNT PLAN]
- Capital constraints: [BOOTSTRAPPED / RAISE CAPITAL]

## Analysis Instructions
Include:
- Startup costs and monthly opex
- Base/upside/downside revenue scenarios
- Margin assumptions and break-even estimate
- Monthly cash runway view
- Hiring and compensation assumptions
- Capital required and use-of-funds plan
- Funding path options (bootstrapped/debt/angel/VC/grants)

## Required Output
1. Year-1 month-by-month forecast summary
2. Year-2 and Year-3 annual projections
3. Break-even timeline and key levers
4. Minimum capital needed for next milestone
5. Investor-ready funding narrative + KPI targets
