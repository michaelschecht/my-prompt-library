# Market Research Plan (Based on ESOMAR)

**Prompt:**
"Design a market research plan for [Category] following ESOMAR guidelines.
Include:
1. Research objectives and hypotheses.
2. Methodology (qual + quant) with sampling plan.
3. Questionnaire outline and bias controls.
4. Data analysis approach and deliverables.
5. Ethics and data privacy considerations.

Output a research brief and timeline."
