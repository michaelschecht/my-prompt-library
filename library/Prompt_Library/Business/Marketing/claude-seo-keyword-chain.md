# Claude SEO Keyword Research Chain

## Purpose
This prompt utilizes a chain-of-thought strategy designed specifically for Claude 3.5 Sonnet to perform deep and structured SEO keyword research, analyzing user intent, competition, and search volume to identify the most valuable long-tail opportunities.

## Instructions
I need to conduct comprehensive keyword research for my [Business Type: e.g., B2B SaaS company, e-commerce store] targeting the primary keyword: "[Primary Keyword: e.g., CRM software, sustainable running shoes]".

Walk me through this step-by-step:

1. **Intent Analysis:** First, analyze the core search intent behind this keyword and identify what users are truly seeking (informational, navigational, transactional).
2. **Competitive Landscape:** Next, break down the competitive landscape by examining what types of content currently rank (listicles, product pages, long-form guides).
3. **Long-Tail Ideation:** Then, generate related long-tail variations that align with different user journey stages (awareness, consideration, decision).
4. **Opportunity Evaluation:** After that, evaluate each keyword opportunity by balancing perceived search volume potential against ranking difficulty.
5. **Final Prioritization:** Finally, prioritize the top 5 keywords with detailed reasoning for why each represents the best opportunity for my specific business goals.

Please provide a structured response addressing each step sequentially.

## Output Format
A step-by-step markdown response broken down into sections matching the steps in the prompt, culminating in a prioritized top 5 keyword list with justifications.

## Example
I need to conduct comprehensive keyword research for my local bakery targeting the primary keyword: "gluten-free custom cakes Austin". Walk me through this step-by-step... (includes the 5 step process outlined above).
