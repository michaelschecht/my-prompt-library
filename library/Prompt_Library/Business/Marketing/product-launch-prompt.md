# Product Launch Playbook (Based on Pragmatic Marketing)

**Prompt:**
"Build a product launch playbook for [Product] based on Pragmatic Marketing principles.
Include:
1. Market problems and positioning.
2. Launch goals and KPIs.
3. Cross-functional RACI and timeline.
4. Asset checklist (sales enablement, PR, web).
5. Post-launch measurement and iteration plan.

Provide a launch calendar and risk log."
