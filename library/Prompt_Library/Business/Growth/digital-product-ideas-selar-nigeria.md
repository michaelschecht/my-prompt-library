# Digital Product Ideas

## Purpose
Use this prompt to brainstorm beginner-friendly digital product opportunities with clear customer pain points and practical go-to-market direction.

## Instructions
Act as a digital marketing expert.

Create 10 beginner-friendly digital product ideas I can sell on Selar in Nigeria.

For each idea:
- Explain it in simple terms
- State the exact problem it solves
- Identify a likely target buyer
- Suggest a starter pricing range in NGN
- Suggest one fast validation test I can run this week

Prioritize ideas that are low-cost to create, solve real local needs, and can be marketed by a solo founder.

## Output Format
Provide a table with columns:
- **Idea**
- **What It Is (Simple Explanation)**
- **Problem It Solves**
- **Target Buyer**
- **Starter Price (NGN)**
- **1-Week Validation Test**

Then add:
- **Top 3 ideas to start with**
- **Why these 3 are best for beginners**

## Example
One idea can be a niche business template pack (e.g., invoice + quotation + receipt bundle) for small service businesses.
