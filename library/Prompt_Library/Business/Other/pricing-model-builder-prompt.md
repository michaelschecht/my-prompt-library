# Prompt: Pricing Model Builder for AX-Platform

## Objective
Generate three distinct pricing models for the AX-Platform, an advanced AI development and deployment platform. For each model, provide a detailed breakdown of its structure, enumerate the pros and cons, and identify the ideal customer segment it would best serve.

## Pricing Models to Propose

### 1. Seat-Based (Per-User) Model
*   **Description:** A straightforward model where customers pay a flat fee per user (or "seat") per month/year. Detail different tiers (e.g., Basic, Pro, Enterprise) with varying feature access and support levels.
*   **Pros:** Predictable revenue, simple to understand and sell, encourages adoption within teams.
*   **Cons:** Can become expensive for large teams, may not reflect actual usage, potential for seat sharing.
*   **Best-Fit Segments:** Small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs), teams with predictable user counts, companies prioritizing budget stability.

### 2. Usage-Based (Pay-as-you-go) Model
*   **Description:** A flexible model where customers are billed based on their consumption of platform resources. Define the key metrics for billing (e.g., API calls, compute hours, data storage, model training time).
*   **Pros:** Scales with customer usage, low barrier to entry, aligns cost with value.
*   **Cons:** Unpredictable costs for customers, complex to track and bill, potential for revenue volatility.
*   **Best-Fit Segments:** Startups, individual developers, businesses with fluctuating or seasonal workloads, customers testing the platform.

### 3. Hybrid Model
*   **Description:** A model that combines elements of both seat-based and usage-based pricing. Propose a structure that includes a base subscription fee for a certain number of seats and a block of included usage, with overage charges for additional users or consumption.
*   **Pros:** Balances predictability with flexibility, captures value from both light and heavy users, offers a path for customers to grow.
*   **Cons:** Can be more complex to communicate, may require sophisticated metering and billing systems.
*   **Best-Fit Segments:** Enterprise customers, businesses with diverse user needs, companies looking for a scalable and predictable pricing structure.

## Desired Output Format
Present the analysis for each model in a clear, structured format. Use headings, bullet points, and bold text to enhance readability. The final output should be a comprehensive document that can be used by the product and sales teams to make informed decisions about the go-to-market strategy for the AX-Platform.
