# Enterprise Risk Assessment (Based on ISO 31000)

**Prompt:**
"Perform an enterprise risk assessment for [Company] aligned with ISO 31000.
Include:
1. Risk taxonomy and context setting.
2. Risk register with likelihood/impact scoring.
3. Heat map and prioritization rationale.
4. Treatment plans (avoid/mitigate/transfer/accept).
5. Monitoring indicators and governance owners.

Output a concise executive summary plus detailed register."
