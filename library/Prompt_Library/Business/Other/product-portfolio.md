# Product Portfolio Optimization (Based on BCG Matrix and Ansoff)

**Prompt:**
"Analyze [Company]’s product portfolio using the BCG matrix and Ansoff growth framework.
Provide:
1. Portfolio mapping with market growth/share assumptions.
