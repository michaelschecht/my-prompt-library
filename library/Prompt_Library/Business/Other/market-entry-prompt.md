# Market Entry Strategy (Based on IMF/World Bank indicators and Kotler)

**Prompt:**
"Develop a market entry strategy for [Product/Service] into [Country/Region]. Use:
- World Bank/IMF macro indicators (GDP growth, inflation, currency stability)
- Kotler’s market segmentation and positioning guidance
- A basic PESTLE analysis

Deliver:
1. Market attractiveness scorecard (weights + scores).
2. Segment prioritization and positioning statement.
3. Recommended entry mode (JV, distributor, direct, acquisition) with rationale.
4. Regulatory and compliance checklist.
5. 12-month go-to-market plan with milestones, budget ranges, and KPIs."
