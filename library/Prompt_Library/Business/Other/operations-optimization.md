# Operations Optimization (Based on Lean/Six Sigma and APQC)

**Prompt:**
"Using Lean, Six Sigma (DMAIC), and APQC process classification, diagnose and optimize [Process].
Deliverables:
1. SIPOC diagram and current-state map.
2. Baseline metrics (cycle time, defect rate, throughput).
3. Root-cause analysis (5 Whys/Fishbone) tied to data.
4. Future-state process design and expected gains.
5. Control plan with KPIs and governance cadence."
