# Strategic Planning Synthesis (Based on Kaplan & Norton Balanced Scorecard, Porter, and HBR)

**Prompt:**
"You are a strategy consultant. Using the Balanced Scorecard (Kaplan & Norton), Porter’s Five Forces, and HBR’s strategy/fit guidance, create a 3-year strategic plan for [Company].

Include:
1. **Context & Diagnosis**: Market size, growth rates, competitive intensity (Porter), and current positioning.
2. **Strategic Objectives**: 3–5 objectives mapped to Balanced Scorecard perspectives (Financial, Customer, Internal Processes, Learning & Growth).
3. **Strategic Initiatives**: 2–4 initiatives per objective with owners, dependencies, and a high-level roadmap.
4. **Assumptions & Risks**: Key assumptions, scenario triggers, and mitigation plans.
5. **Metrics & Targets**: SMART KPIs tied to each initiative.

Output as a structured report with tables where helpful and cite the specific framework elements used."
