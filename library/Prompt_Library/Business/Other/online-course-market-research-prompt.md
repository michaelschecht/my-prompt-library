# Market Research Prompt for an Online Course

## 1. Course Idea & Transformation

**Course Idea:**
*   [What is the specific topic of your online course? What skills will it teach?]

**Student Transformation:**
*   [What is the "before" and "after" for your ideal student? What transformation will they experience after completing your course?]

## 2. Ideal Student Profile

**Demographics:**
*   [Age, gender, location, job title, income level, education.]

**Psychographics:**
*   **Goals:** [What are their career or personal goals? What are they trying to achieve?]
*   **Challenges:** [What are the biggest challenges or pain points they face related to your course topic?]
*   **Watering Holes:** [Where do they hang out online? (e.g., specific blogs, forums, social media groups, subreddits, YouTube channels)]
*   **Current Solutions:** [What are they currently doing to try and solve their problems? (e.g., reading blog posts, watching YouTube videos, using other tools or courses)]

## 3. Competitive Landscape

**Direct Competitors (Other Courses):**
*   [List 3-5 other online courses on the same topic.]
    *   Course 1: [URL]
    *   Course 2: [URL]
    *   ...

**Indirect Competitors (Alternative Solutions):**
*   [List 2-3 alternative solutions. (e.g., books, workshops, in-person classes, free content like blogs or YouTube channels)]
    *   Alternative 1: [URL]
    *   ...

**For each competitor, analyze:**
*   **Strengths:** [What are their strengths? What do students praise in their reviews?]
*   **Weaknesses:** [What are their weaknesses? What do students complain about?]
*   **Price & Business Model:** [What is the price? Is it a one-time purchase, subscription, etc.?]
*   **Marketing & Sales Funnel:** [How are they attracting students? (e.g., free webinar, email course, paid ads)]

## 4. Market & Keyword Research

**Keyword Research:**
*   [List 5-10 keywords your ideal student might search for on Google or YouTube when looking for a solution to their problem.]

**Content Gap Analysis:**
*   [Based on your competitor and keyword research, are there any topics that are not being covered in-depth? Are there any unanswered questions your target audience has?]

## 5. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

**Your Unique Angle:**
*   [Based on your research, what makes your course different and better than the competition? (e.g., your unique teaching style, a more hands-on approach, a focus on a specific niche, a more affordable price)]

---

**Instructions for the AI:**

Based on the information provided above, please generate a comprehensive market research report for the online course idea. The report should include:

*   A detailed analysis of the ideal student profile.
*   An in-depth review of the competitive landscape.
*   An analysis of market and keyword research to identify content gaps.
*   A clear and compelling Unique Selling Proposition (USP) for the course.
*   Recommendations for pricing, marketing, and a launch strategy.
