# Pricing Strategy Framework (Based on Nagle & Holden, and OECD guidance)

**Prompt:**
"Design a pricing strategy for [Product/Service] using value-based pricing (Nagle & Holden) and competitive reference pricing. Include:
1. Customer value drivers and willingness-to-pay hypotheses.
2. Price architecture (tiers, bundles, add-ons).
3. Competitive benchmark table with feature parity notes.
4. OECD-style transfer pricing considerations (if applicable).
5. Sensitivity analysis for margin, volume, and churn.

Provide a final pricing recommendation with guardrails and a rollout plan."
