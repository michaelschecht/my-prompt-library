# AI & Tech Stock Deep Dive

**Schedule:** Every Friday — 4:30 PM
**Category:** Finance
**Tools Required:** Notion, Web Search
**Estimated Time:** ~8 minutes

## Prompt

Search for this week's key news and analyst activity for AI and tech sector holdings. Focus on:
- Any analyst upgrades/downgrades or price target changes
- Significant product announcements or partnership news
- Macro events affecting the sector (Fed commentary, trade policy, chip export rules)
- Insider buying/selling above $1M in value

Cross-reference against my Notion portfolio. Flag any position where the investment thesis has materially changed this week. Produce a "Week in Review" for my tech/AI positions — 3–5 bullets per notable name, and a sector sentiment score (Bullish / Neutral / Cautious) with one-line rationale.
