# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) Builder

## Purpose
Generate mutual or one-way NDAs with scope, exclusions, duration, and remedy language suited to deal context.

## Instructions
Act as a contracts specialist and draft an NDA.

Inputs:
- **Parties:** [disclosing/receiving]
- **NDA type:** [mutual/one-way]
- **Purpose of disclosure:** [project/deal]
- **Confidential info definition:** [broad/narrow + examples]
- **Term:** [years]
- **Return/destruction obligations:** [requirements]
- **Jurisdiction:** [state/country]

Include carve-outs, compelled disclosure process, injunctive relief language, and residual knowledge treatment.

## Output Format
- Final NDA draft
- Negotiation checklist (must-have vs flexible clauses)
