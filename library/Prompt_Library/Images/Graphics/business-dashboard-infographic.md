# Business Dashboard Infographic

## Purpose
Create a realistic or stylized graphic of a business analytics dashboard to showcase data-driven decision making in corporate presentations or marketing materials.

## Instructions
Design an infographic or UI mockup showing a comprehensive business dashboard. It should include various charts (bar graphs, line charts, pie charts), key performance indicators (KPIs), and a clean, modern interface. The tone should be professional, corporate, and clean.

## Output Format
Provide the prompt for an AI image generator to create the business dashboard graphic, specifying colors, layout type, and chart elements.

## Example
**Image Generation Prompt:** "A modern, sleek UI design of a business analytics dashboard displayed on a clean white background. Features include colorful bar charts, a rising line graph, and circular KPI indicators. Professional corporate color palette with blues, greens, and crisp whites. Flat design, isometric view, high-quality vector style."
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 3:2
**Style:** UI Mockup, Flat Design, Corporate
