# Fintech App Minimalist Logo

## Purpose
Generate a highly recognizable, minimalist app icon and logo suitable for a modern financial technology application.

## Instructions
Design a clean, simple, and memorable logo for a fintech app. It should visually suggest finance, growth, or secure transactions (e.g., stylized coins, upward arrows, shields, or abstract geometric 'S' shapes). The design must work well at small sizes (like an iOS/Android app icon). Use a vibrant but trustworthy color scheme, such as emerald green or deep blue.

## Output Format
Provide the prompt for an AI image generator to create the fintech app logo, specifying the central motif, the requirement for simplicity, and app icon compatibility.

## Example
**Image Generation Prompt:** "A minimalist app icon logo for a modern fintech company. The design features a stylized, continuous line art of an upward arrow turning into a subtle coin or leaf shape, symbolizing financial growth. Emerald green and dark navy blue on a clean white background. Flat vector, highly scalable, simple geometry, trustworthy and modern."
**Aspect Ratio:** 1:1
**Style:** Minimalist App Icon, Line Art, Flat Vector
