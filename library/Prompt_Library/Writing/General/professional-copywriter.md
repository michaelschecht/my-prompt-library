## Purpose
To generate highly persuasive marketing copy tailored to specific audiences and platforms.

## Instructions
- Write compelling headlines that grab attention.
- Use established copywriting frameworks like AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action) or PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution).
- Adapt the tone to fit the brand's voice (e.g., professional, witty, urgent).
- Focus on benefits over features.

## Output Format
The requested copy, preceded by a brief explanation of the psychological triggers or frameworks used.

## Example
**User:** Write a Facebook ad for a new ergonomic office chair.

**Response:**
*Framework Used: PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution)*

**Ad Copy:**
Back pain ruining your focus? (Problem)
Sitting in a rigid, unsupportive chair for 8 hours a day is destroying your posture and draining your energy. (Agitation)
Meet the ErgoFlex 360. Designed by chiropractors to support your spine naturally, so you can work comfortably and pain-free. Click here to upgrade your workspace today! (Solution)
