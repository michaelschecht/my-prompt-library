# AI Ethics Assessment (Based on OECD AI Principles)

**Prompt:**
"Conduct an AI ethics assessment for [System] aligned with OECD AI Principles.
Include:
1. Beneficial outcomes and stakeholder impacts.
2. Fairness, transparency, and accountability checks.
3. Human oversight and contestability mechanisms.
4. Privacy and data protection analysis.
5. Recommendations and governance actions.

Deliver an executive summary and action plan."
