# AI Virtual Assistant Prompts

This document provides a set of prompts for an AI agent to act as a virtual assistant, managing emails, scheduling appointments, and organizing tasks.

## 1. Email Management

### Prompt: Summarize my inbox

**Objective:** Get a quick overview of new and important emails.

**Prompt:**
"Summarize my inbox for today. Categorize emails into 'Urgent', 'Requires Response', and 'For Your Information'. For each email, include the sender, subject, and a one-sentence summary."

### Prompt: Draft a reply to an email

**Objective:** Quickly respond to a common inquiry.

**Prompt:**
"Draft a reply to the email from [Sender] with the subject '[Subject]'. The reply should acknowledge receipt of the email and inform them that I will get back to them within 24 hours. My tone should be professional and friendly."

### Prompt: Find all emails from a specific person

**Objective:** Locate all correspondence from a particular contact.

**Prompt:**
"Find all emails from [Sender Name/Email Address] in my inbox. List the subject lines and dates of the last 5 emails."

## 2. Scheduling Appointments

### Prompt: Schedule a new meeting

**Objective:** Arrange a meeting with one or more people.

**Prompt:**
"Schedule a 30-minute meeting with [Participant Name(s)/Email(s)] to discuss '[Meeting Topic]'. Propose three alternative time slots for next week, between 9 AM and 5 PM [Your Timezone]. The meeting should be a video conference. Include a brief agenda in the meeting invitation:
- Introduction (5 mins)
- Discussion of [Topic] (20 mins)
- Next Steps (5 mins)"

### Prompt: Reschedule an existing meeting

**Objective:** Move a scheduled meeting to a new time.

**Prompt:**
"Reschedule my meeting with [Participant Name(s)] about '[Meeting Topic]' from [Original Date and Time] to [New Date and Time]. Send an updated invitation to all participants and include a brief note explaining the change."

### Prompt: Show my schedule for the day

**Objective:** Get an overview of the day's appointments.

**Prompt:**
"Show me my schedule for today, [Date]. List all my appointments with their times, participants, and locations/meeting links."

## 3. Task Organization

### Prompt: Create a to-do list

**Objective:** Organize tasks for the day or week.

**Prompt:**
"Create a to-do list for me for this week. Include the following tasks:
- [Task 1] (Due: [Date])
- [Task 2] (Due: [Date])
- [Task 3] (Due: [Date])
Organize the tasks by priority: High, Medium, and Low."

### Prompt: Set a reminder

**Objective:** Get a notification for an important task or event.

**Prompt:**
"Set a reminder for me to '[Task]' on [Date] at [Time]. The reminder should be sent to me via email."

### Prompt: Prioritize my tasks

**Objective:** Determine which tasks to focus on first.

**Prompt:**
"I have the following tasks: [List of tasks]. Help me prioritize them based on their urgency and importance. Provide a ranked list with a brief explanation for the ranking of each task."
