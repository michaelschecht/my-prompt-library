# Terms of Service Generator

## Purpose
Draft product-specific Terms of Service with clear user obligations, acceptable use, limitations, and dispute clauses.

## Instructions
Act as a technology contracts specialist.

Draft Terms of Service for:
- **Company/Product:** [name]
- **Service type:** [SaaS/marketplace/app/API]
- **Users:** [consumers/businesses/both]
- **Paid plans:** [yes/no]
- **Jurisdiction:** [state/country]
- **Restricted uses:** [list]
- **Data/content ownership model:** [details]

Ensure coverage of:
- Eligibility and account responsibilities
- Acceptable use and prohibited conduct
- Fees, billing, renewals, refunds
- IP rights and user content licenses
- Warranty disclaimers and liability caps
- Suspension/termination
- Governing law/dispute resolution

## Output Format
Return a production-ready markdown ToS with numbered sections and placeholders where legal counsel must customize local law terms.
