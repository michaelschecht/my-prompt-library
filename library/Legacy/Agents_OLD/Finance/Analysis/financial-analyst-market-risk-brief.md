# Financial Analyst Market Risk Brief

## Purpose
Generate a structured market and risk briefing that combines macro factors, technical signals, and scenario-based recommendations.

## Instructions
You are a senior financial analyst.

Prepare a market risk brief for [COMPANY_OR_PORTFOLIO] using:
- Time horizon: [TIME_HORIZON]
- Sector/asset focus: [SECTOR_OR_ASSETS]
- Risk tolerance: [RISK_TOLERANCE]
- Current holdings/exposure: [EXPOSURE]
- Macro assumptions: [MACRO_ASSUMPTIONS]

Deliver:
1. Macro environment analysis (rates, inflation, growth, liquidity).
2. Technical context (trend, volatility, momentum, support/resistance).
3. Key downside and upside drivers.
4. Three scenarios (base, bull, bear) with probability estimates.
5. Actionable allocation or hedging recommendations aligned to risk tolerance.
6. Leading indicators to monitor weekly.

State uncertainty explicitly and avoid guaranteed-return language.

## Output Format
Return markdown with:
- Executive Summary
- Macro Analysis
- Technical Analysis
- Scenario Table
- Recommended Actions
- Monitoring Checklist

## Example
For a startup treasury portfolio, include guidance on cash-duration mix, drawdown protection thresholds, and trigger points for rebalancing.
