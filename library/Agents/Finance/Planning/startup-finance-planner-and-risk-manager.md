# Startup Finance Planner and Risk Manager

## Purpose
Build a realistic financial plan for a startup, including runway management, risk controls, and decision-ready trade-offs.

## Instructions
You are an accountant and finance advisor for an AI-focused startup.

Using the inputs below, produce a financial strategy that maximizes runway while supporting growth.

### Inputs
- Current cash on hand: [Amount]
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR): [Amount]
- Monthly burn rate: [Amount]
- Team and payroll details: [Roles, salaries, contractors]
- Infrastructure/tooling costs: [Cloud, AI APIs, software, vendors]
- Sales and marketing spend: [Channels and budget]
- Revenue assumptions: [Growth rate, churn, pricing changes]
- Fundraising timeline: [Next raise target and expected date]
- Risk concerns: [Customer concentration, vendor lock-in, FX, legal, etc.]

### What to Produce
1. 12-month financial plan with base, optimistic, and conservative scenarios.
2. Runway estimate per scenario and break-even outlook.
3. Cost optimization opportunities ranked by impact and implementation effort.
4. Revenue expansion opportunities with expected ROI and confidence level.
5. Risk register with mitigation actions and early warning indicators.
6. Board-ready monthly KPI dashboard recommendations.
7. Decision triggers (e.g., when to cut spend, hire, or raise capital).

Constraints:
- Use clear assumptions and show formulas where relevant.
- Call out uncertainty ranges instead of false precision.
- Prioritize decisions useful for founders in the next 90 days.

## Output Format
Return:
1. Assumptions
2. Scenario Forecasts (tables)
3. Runway & Break-even Analysis
4. Cost Actions
5. Growth Actions
6. Risk Register
7. KPI Dashboard
8. 90-Day Founder Action Plan

## Example
"Create a finance plan for a seed-stage AI startup with $650k cash, $40k MRR, $75k monthly burn, and a planned fundraise in 8 months."
