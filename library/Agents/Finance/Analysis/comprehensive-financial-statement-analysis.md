# Comprehensive Financial Statement Analysis

<!-- Performs an in-depth analysis of a company's financial statements including income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow. -->

- **Category:** Finance

## Purpose

To provide a thorough evaluation of a company's financial performance by analyzing key ratios, trends, and financial health indicators over multiple periods.

---

## Input Requirements

**Required:**
- [COMPANY NAME]: The name or ticker of the company.
- [FINANCIAL STATEMENTS]: The income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement data (or link to 10-K/10-Q).
- [TIMEFRAME]: The period being analyzed (e.g., Annual 2023, Q3 2023).

**Optional:**
- [INDUSTRY PEERS]: Competitors for comparative analysis.
- [SPECIFIC CONCERNS]: Areas to focus on (e.g., debt levels, margin compression).

---

## Prompt

```
You are an expert financial analyst. Please analyze the financial statements of [COMPANY NAME] for [TIMEFRAME]. \n\nReview the provided [FINANCIAL STATEMENTS] and provide a comprehensive analysis. \nCalculate key financial ratios (liquidity, solvency, profitability, and efficiency). \nIdentify major trends in revenue growth, margin expansion/contraction, and cash generation. \nHighlight any red flags or areas of concern. \n[If provided, compare performance against INDUSTRY PEERS: [INDUSTRY PEERS]].\n[Focus especially on: [SPECIFIC CONCERNS]].\n\nEnsure your analysis is professional, objective, and structured logically.
```

---

## Expected Output

A detailed report covering the financial health, key metrics, and strategic insights for the company.

**Format:**
- Markdown document with clear headings and bulleted lists
- Executive Summary, Ratio Analysis, Trend Analysis, Red Flags, Conclusion

**Example structure:**
1. Executive Summary\n2. Profitability Analysis\n3. Liquidity & Solvency\n4. Efficiency & Operations\n5. Key Risks & Red Flags\n6. Conclusion & Outlook
