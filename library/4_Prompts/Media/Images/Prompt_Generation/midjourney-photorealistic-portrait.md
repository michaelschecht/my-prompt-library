# Midjourney Photorealistic Portrait

## Purpose
This prompt is designed to generate highly realistic, studio-quality portraits using Midjourney v6. It incorporates professional photography terminology to ensure accurate lighting, texturing, and details.

## Instructions
A photorealistic close-up portrait of a [Subject Description: age, ethnicity, expression], featuring detailed skin texture, pores, and [Specific Facial Features like freckles or subtle wrinkles]. Captured with a [Camera Lens: e.g., 50mm f/1.8, 85mm f/1.2] lens, using [Lighting Style: e.g., cinematic lighting, dramatic rembrandt lighting, soft natural sunlight]. The background is [Background Description: e.g., a blurred urban street, dark studio backdrop, soft bokeh], keeping the focus entirely on the subject. Ultra-detailed, 8K resolution, octane render, hyper-realistic. --v 6.0 --style raw

## Output Format
A visually stunning, high-resolution image showcasing a photorealistic human subject.

## Example
A photorealistic close-up portrait of a 45-year-old Scandinavian man with a rugged beard and intense gaze, featuring detailed skin texture, pores, and subtle smile lines around the eyes. Captured with an 85mm f/1.2 lens, using dramatic rembrandt lighting. The background is a soft, dark studio backdrop, keeping the focus entirely on the subject. Ultra-detailed, 8K resolution, octane render, hyper-realistic. --v 6.0 --style raw
