# Midjourney Architectural Prompter

## Purpose
Generates highly detailed and professional prompts for Midjourney to create realistic architectural visualizations, exterior designs, and interior spaces.

## Instructions
1. Describe the core subject (e.g., modern cabin, brutalist office building, cozy scandinavian living room).
2. Specify the environment and lighting (e.g., snowy forest at dusk, golden hour in a bustling city).
3. Detail the materials and architectural style (e.g., concrete and glass, biophilic design, minimalist).
4. Specify the camera angle or perspective (e.g., wide angle, aerial view, eye-level).

## Output Format
A comma-separated prompt optimized for Midjourney v6, including subject, environment, lighting, camera settings, style keywords, and aspect ratio parameters.

## Example

**User Prompt:**
"I need a prompt for a modern, eco-friendly library situated in a dense forest. Use lots of glass and wood. It should be daytime with sunlight filtering through the trees. Wide shot."

**AI Response:**
`/imagine prompt: An ultra-realistic architectural visualization of a modern eco-friendly library nestled in a dense, lush green forest, wide angle exterior shot. The building features expansive floor-to-ceiling glass walls and sustainable timber cladding, seamlessly blending with nature. Biophilic design, green roof. Sunlight filtering through the forest canopy, creating dappled volumetric lighting on the facade. Photorealistic, 8k resolution, architectural photography, shot on 35mm lens, highly detailed, Unreal Engine 5 render style, serene atmosphere --ar 16:9 --v 6.0 --style raw`

## Tips for Use
- Midjourney v6 prefers more natural language, but specific architectural keywords (e.g., "brutalist," "cantilever," "volumetric lighting") still heavily influence the output.
- Experiment with different aspect ratios (`--ar 16:9` for landscape/exterior, `--ar 4:5` for portrait/interior) based on your needs.
