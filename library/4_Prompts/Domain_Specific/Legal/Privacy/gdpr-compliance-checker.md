# GDPR Compliance Checker

## Purpose
Review a product, process, or policy for GDPR alignment and produce a risk-ranked remediation plan mapped to key GDPR obligations.

## Instructions
I want you to act as an EU privacy compliance specialist.

Evaluate the following organization/process for GDPR compliance:
- **Organization:** [name]
- **Business model:** [SaaS/ecommerce/etc.]
- **Data categories processed:** [list]
- **Data subjects:** [EU users/employees/etc.]
- **Processing purposes:** [list]
- **Legal bases currently used:** [list]
- **Third-party processors:** [list]
- **Cross-border transfers:** [yes/no + details]
- **Current controls:** [DPA, RoPA, DPIA, DSAR workflow, breach plan, etc.]

Assess compliance against:
- Lawfulness, fairness, transparency
- Purpose limitation, data minimization, retention limits
- Accuracy, integrity/confidentiality, accountability
- Data subject rights handling (access, deletion, portability, objection)
- Processor management and transfer safeguards (SCCs/adequacy)
- Breach response readiness (Articles 33/34)

## Output Format
- **Executive Summary** (overall posture: Low/Moderate/High risk)
- **Findings Table** with columns:
  - GDPR area
  - Current state
  - Gap
  - Severity (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
  - Recommended fix
  - Owner
  - Target date
- **Top 10 Priority Actions (30/60/90 days)**
- **Evidence Checklist** (documents/artifacts to collect)

## Tips for Use
- Provide real process details, not policy-only text.
- Attach your actual privacy policy and DPA snippets for better output.
- Re-run after remediation to track progress.

## Reputable Sources
- GDPR full text: https://gdpr-info.eu/
- EDPB guidance portal: https://www.edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/our-documents/guidelines_en
- ICO accountability framework: https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/accountability-framework/
