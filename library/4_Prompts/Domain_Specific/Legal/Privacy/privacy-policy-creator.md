# Privacy Policy Creator

## Purpose
Create a jurisdiction-aware privacy policy covering data collection, use, sharing, retention, rights, and contact channels.

## Instructions
Act as a privacy policy drafting assistant.

Generate a privacy policy for:
- **Organization:** [name]
- **Regions served:** [EU/US/UK/Global]
- **Data collected:** [list]
- **Purposes:** [list]
- **Cookies/trackers:** [tools]
- **Processors/subprocessors:** [list]
- **Transfers outside origin country:** [details]
- **User rights request channel:** [email/form]

Include optional modules for GDPR and CPRA where relevant.

## Output Format
- Plain-language policy with clear headings
- Data categories table
- Legal basis table (if GDPR applies)
- Consumer rights section by region
- Retention and security summary
