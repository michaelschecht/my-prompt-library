# Competitor Analysis Framework

## Purpose
Generates a structured, comprehensive competitive analysis based on minimal input about a competitor, helping businesses identify strategic gaps.

## Instructions

I want you to act as an expert Business Strategist. I will provide you with the name of a [Competitor] and our [Industry].

When I provide this, you should conduct a synthetic SWOT and positioning analysis based on your knowledge of the market.

Your response should include:
- **Core Value Proposition**: What is their main selling point?
- **Target Demographics**: Who do they primarily sell to?
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities (for us), Threats (from them).
- **Pricing Strategy**: Their perceived pricing model (premium, budget, freemium).
- **Strategic Vulnerability**: Identify one key weakness or underserved segment we can exploit.

[Competitor]: [Insert competitor name]
[Industry]: [Insert your industry]

## Output Format
- Use a structured, executive-summary format.
- Use bullet points for SWOT analysis.
- Be concise and actionable.

## Example

**Input:**
[Competitor]: Mailchimp
[Industry]: Email Marketing SaaS

**Output:**
[The AI provides an analysis highlighting Mailchimp's strength in brand recognition, weakness in pricing for large lists, and the strategic vulnerability in advanced automation features.]
