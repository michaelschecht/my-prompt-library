# Risk, Compliance, and Contingency Plan

## Objective
Build a practical risk and compliance framework for **[BUSINESS TYPE]** in **[REGION]**.

## Inputs
- Business type: [INDUSTRY]
- Geography: [COUNTRY/STATE]
- Data sensitivity: [LOW/MED/HIGH]
- Operational dependencies: [VENDORS/PLATFORMS]

## Analysis Instructions
Cover:
- High-level legal/regulatory obligations
- Privacy/security requirements
- Financial, operational, and reputational risk register
- Incident response considerations
- Business continuity scenarios

## Required Output
1. Prioritized risk matrix (impact × likelihood)
2. Must-have compliance checklist
3. Incident response playbook outline
4. 3 contingency scenarios with responses
5. Ownership model and review cadence

## Guardrail
Provide strategic and operational guidance only; do not provide legal advice.
