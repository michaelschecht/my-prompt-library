# Pricing and Unit Economics Plan

## Objective
Design a pricing strategy for **[BUSINESS]** that supports growth and profitability.

## Inputs
- Product/service: [DESCRIPTION]
- Customer segments: [SEGMENTS]
- Cost structure notes: [COGS/OPEX]
- Pricing constraints: [COMPETITIVE / BRAND POSITIONING]

## Analysis Instructions
Evaluate:
- Pricing model choices (one-time, subscription, tiered, usage-based)
- Segment-level willingness-to-pay
- Contribution margin assumptions
- LTV/CAC target ranges
- Packaging, discounts, and promo approach

## Required Output
1. Recommended pricing structure + rationale
2. Unit economics summary (base/upside/downside)
3. Break-even customer volume targets
4. Profitability levers and tradeoffs
5. 60-day pricing test plan
