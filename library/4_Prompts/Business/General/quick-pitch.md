## 30-Second Elevator Pitch

**Structure:**
1. **Hook** (5 sec): Grab attention with problem or stat
2. **Solution** (10 sec): What you do and for whom
3. **Traction/Proof** (10 sec): Key metric or achievement
4. **Call to Action** (5 sec): What's next?
