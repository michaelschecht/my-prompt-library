# Meeting Agenda Optimizer

## Purpose
Transforms vague meeting topics into structured, outcome-oriented agendas to improve operational efficiency and reduce wasted time.

## Instructions

I want you to act as an Executive Assistant and Productivity Expert. I have a meeting coming up and need a highly structured, effective agenda.

Meeting Goal: [What must be achieved?]
Duration: [e.g., 45 minutes]
Attendees: [List roles or names]
Rough Topics: [List the messy topics you want to cover]

Please provide:
1. A structured agenda with timeboxed sections.
2. The specific outcome required for each section.
3. Pre-read materials or preparation required from attendees.
4. A suggested format for capturing action items during the meeting.

## Output Format
Clear, formatted text with time allocations (e.g., "5 mins - Topic").

## Example
**Input:** Goal: Decide on Q3 OKRs. 60 mins. Leadership team. Topics: review last quarter, brainstorm new ones, finalize top 3.
**Output:** [Creates a strict 60-min breakdown allocating 10 mins to review, 25 mins to brainstorm, and 25 mins to finalize, specifying that attendees must bring 2 drafted OKRs to the meeting.]

## Tips for Use
Always define a clear "Meeting Goal". If you can't define the goal, the prompt might suggest canceling the meeting!
