# Vendor Evaluation Matrix Builder

## Purpose
Assists operations and procurement teams in objectively evaluating and comparing different vendors or software solutions.

## Instructions

I want you to act as a Procurement Specialist. I need to evaluate multiple vendors for [Software/Service/Product Type].

Here are the vendors I am considering:
[List of Vendors]

Here are my primary requirements and constraints:
[List requirements, e.g., Budget < $10k, SOC2 compliance, 24/7 support]

Please generate:
1. A comprehensive evaluation matrix (in markdown table format) comparing these vendors based on standard industry criteria and my specific requirements.
2. A list of 5 critical questions I should ask each vendor during the demo/negotiation phase.
3. Potential red flags to watch out for with this type of service.

## Output Format
Markdown table for the matrix, followed by bulleted lists for questions and red flags.

## Example
**Input:** Evaluating CRM vendors: Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive. Req: budget friendly for 10 users, easy email integration.
**Output:** [Generates a table comparing features, pricing tiers, ease of use, followed by probing questions about hidden fees and data export capabilities.]

## Tips for Use
Be specific about your budget and "must-have" features to get a highly tailored matrix.
