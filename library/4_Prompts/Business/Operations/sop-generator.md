# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Generator

## Purpose
This prompt assists operations managers in creating clear, standardized, and easy-to-follow Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for various business processes.

## Instructions
1. Identify the process that needs an SOP.
2. Provide a rough outline or bullet points of the current steps.
3. Specify the target audience (e.g., new hires, warehouse staff, customer service reps).
4. Mention any specific tools, software, or compliance requirements involved.

## Output Format
- **Document Title:** [Process Name] SOP
- **Purpose:** Brief statement of why this procedure exists.
- **Scope:** Who and what this procedure applies to.
- **Prerequisites/Tools:** What is needed before starting.
- **Step-by-Step Procedure:** Numbered list of actions, formatted clearly (using bolding for key actions).
- **Troubleshooting/Exceptions:** What to do if things go wrong.

## Example

**User Prompt:**
"Create an SOP for onboarding a new client in our marketing agency.
Steps:
1. Sales closes the deal and signs contract via DocuSign.
2. Account manager creates a folder in Google Drive.
3. Send welcome email with intake questionnaire.
4. Set up client in Asana.
5. Schedule kickoff call.
Audience: Account Managers."

**AI Response:**
**Document Title:** New Client Onboarding SOP

**Purpose:** To ensure a smooth, professional, and consistent onboarding experience for all new marketing agency clients.

**Scope:** Applies to all Account Managers upon the successful closing of a new client contract by the Sales team.

**Prerequisites/Tools:**
- Access to DocuSign
- Google Workspace (Drive, Gmail)
- Asana
- Calendly (or similar scheduling tool)

**Step-by-Step Procedure:**

**Phase 1: Setup & Internal Handoff**
1. **Verify Contract:** Confirm via DocuSign that the client contract is fully executed.
2. **Create Client Folder:**
   - Navigate to the `Clients > Active` directory in Google Drive.
   - Create a new folder formatted as `[Client Name] - [Year]`.
   - Copy the standard sub-folders (Assets, Reports, Contracts) into the new directory.

**Phase 2: Client Communication & Project Setup**
3. **Send Welcome Communication:**
   - Use the "New Client Welcome" email template.
   - Attach the link to the New Client Intake Questionnaire.
   - Send the email to the primary client contact.
4. **Initialize Asana Project:**
   - Open Asana and duplicate the "Standard Client Onboarding Template".
   - Rename the project to `[Client Name] - Onboarding`.
   - Assign initial tasks to relevant team members with due dates.

**Phase 3: Kickoff**
5. **Schedule Kickoff Call:**
   - Once the intake questionnaire is returned, send the client a Calendly link to book the Kickoff Call.
   - Ensure the relevant internal team members are invited to the scheduled calendar event.

**Troubleshooting/Exceptions:**
- If the client does not return the questionnaire within 3 days, send a polite follow-up email.
- If specific custom services were sold, consult the Sales Rep to adjust the Asana template accordingly before the kickoff call.

## Tips for Use
- Keep your initial input as detailed as possible to minimize revisions.
- Ask the AI to include a "Revision History" section if the SOP will be updated frequently.
