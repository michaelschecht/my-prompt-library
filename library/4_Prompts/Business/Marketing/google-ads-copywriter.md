# Google Ads Copywriter

## Purpose
Creates high-converting Google Search Ad copy that strictly adheres to Google's character limits for headlines and descriptions, optimized for specific keywords.

## Instructions
I want you to act as a Google Ads Copywriting Expert. I need you to write ad copy for a Search campaign.

Here are the parameters:
- **Product/Service:** [Product or Service Name]
- **Target Keywords:** [Comma separated list of 3-5 target keywords]
- **Unique Selling Proposition (USP):** [Your USP or current promotion, e.g., "Free Shipping", "20% off for new users"]

Please generate:
1. **15 Headlines:** Maximum 30 characters each. Include a mix of keyword-focused, benefit-driven, and urgency-based headlines.
2. **4 Descriptions:** Maximum 90 characters each. Must include strong Calls to Action (CTAs).
3. **2 Sitelink Extension Ideas:** Short links to provide additional value (e.g., "Pricing", "Case Studies").

**CRITICAL CONSTRAINT:** You must strictly count the characters for every headline and description. If a headline exceeds 30 characters, or a description exceeds 90 characters, you must rewrite it until it fits. Display the character count next to each line (e.g., Headline Text [28/30]).

## Output Format
A structured list categorized by Headlines, Descriptions, and Sitelinks, with character counts explicitly shown.

## Example
**Input Parameters:**
- Product: ProClean CRM
- Target Keywords: CRM for cleaners, cleaning business software, scheduling app
- USP: 14-day free trial, no credit card required

## Tips for Use
- Provide exact keywords you are bidding on to ensure the AI includes them in the headlines to improve your Ad Quality Score.
- Double-check character counts manually, as LLMs occasionally miscount.
