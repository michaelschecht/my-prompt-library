# Product Launch Strategy

## Purpose
This prompt helps entrepreneurs and marketing managers create a comprehensive, phased product launch strategy. It ensures all critical aspects, including timelines, marketing channels, and core messaging, are thoroughly planned out before the product goes live.

## Instructions
I want you to act as an expert Chief Marketing Officer with decades of experience launching successful tech products.

I am launching a new product: [Describe your product in 2-3 sentences, including key features and unique selling propositions].
My primary target audience is: [Describe the demographics and pain points of your target audience].
My main goal for this launch is: [State your specific, measurable goal, e.g., 5000 active users in the first month].
My budget for marketing is: [State your budget].

Based on this information, create a comprehensive product launch strategy.

Your response should include:
- A phased timeline broken down into Pre-Launch (T-minus 4 weeks), Launch Day, and Post-Launch (Days 1-14).
- The top 3 marketing channels I should focus my budget and effort on, with specific tactics for each.
- 3 core messaging pillars that will resonate with my target audience.
- Potential risks and mitigation strategies for the launch.

## Output Format
- Use clear markdown formatting with bold headings for each phase.
- Present the timeline as a bulleted list.
- Keep the strategic advice actionable, concise, and specific to the provided product details.

## Example
**Input:**
Product: A desktop application that uses AI to organize messy computer files automatically.
Target Audience: Freelance designers and video editors who have cluttered hard drives.
Goal: 2000 paid software licenses sold in the first 30 days.
Budget: $5000.

**Output:**
# Product Launch Strategy: AI File Organizer

## Core Messaging Pillars
1. **Time Saved:** "Stop searching, start creating. Reclaim 5 hours a week."
2. **Peace of Mind:** "A clean digital workspace for a clear creative mind."
3. **Security:** "All organization happens locally. Your files never leave your machine."

## Phased Timeline
### Pre-Launch (T-minus 4 weeks)
- Create a landing page offering a 20% discount for early waitlist signups.
- Begin outreach to design influencers on YouTube and TikTok.

### Launch Day
- Announce on Product Hunt.
- Send the launch email to the waitlist.
- Activate $1000 in targeted Facebook Ads.

### Post-Launch (Days 1-14)
- Collect testimonials from early adopters.
- Launch retargeting ads.

## Top 3 Marketing Channels
1. **Influencer Sponsorships:** Sponsor 3 mid-tier design YouTubers.
2. **Product Hunt:** Leverage the tech-savvy early adopter community.
3. **Targeted Social Ads:** Instagram ads showing a satisfying "before and after" of a messy folder being organized.

## Tips for Use
- Be as specific as possible in your product description; the better the input, the more tailored the strategy.
- If you have a zero-dollar budget, explicitly state that so the AI focuses purely on organic growth tactics.
