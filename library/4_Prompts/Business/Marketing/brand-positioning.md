# Brand Positioning Brief (Based on Kotler and Ries/Trout)

**Prompt:**
"Create a brand positioning brief for [Brand] using Kotler’s STP framework and Ries/Trout positioning principles.
Include:
1. Target segments and personas.
2. Category definition and frame of reference.
3. Points of parity and differentiation.
4. Brand promise, RTBs, and tone guidelines.
5. Messaging architecture and tagline options.

Provide a one-page positioning statement and rationale."
