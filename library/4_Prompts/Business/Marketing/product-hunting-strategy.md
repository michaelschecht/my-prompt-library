# Product Hunting Strategy

## Purpose
Generates a structured, week-by-week launch plan for debuting a new software product or feature on Product Hunt to maximize visibility and upvotes.

## Instructions
I want you to act as a Startup Growth Hacker who specializes in Product Hunt launches. I am launching a new product called [Product Name].

Here is what the product does: [Brief Product Description].
Our target audience is: [Target Audience].

Please create a comprehensive 4-week pre-launch and launch-day strategy.

The strategy must include:
1. **Weeks 1-3 (Pre-Launch):** Community building, finding a Hunter, preparing assets (images, video, first comment), and teaser campaigns on social media.
2. **Launch Day (Hour-by-Hour):** Exactly what to do from 12:01 AM PST to 11:59 PM PST to maintain momentum.
3. **Post-Launch:** How to leverage the Product Hunt badge and traffic in the days following the launch.

Include specific examples of what the "Maker's First Comment" should say, and suggest 3 angles for the main tagline.

## Output Format
A detailed timeline formatted as a Markdown document with bulleted action items for each phase.

## Example
**Input Parameters:**
- Product Name: TaskFlow AI
- Description: An AI agent that automatically reads Slack messages and converts action items into Jira tickets.
- Target Audience: Product Managers and Scrum Masters.

## Tips for Use
- Be as specific as possible about your product's unique value proposition.
- Use the generated "Maker's Comment" as a draft, but ensure you add a personal, authentic touch before posting.
