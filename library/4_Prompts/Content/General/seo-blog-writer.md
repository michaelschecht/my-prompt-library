## Purpose
To create engaging, informative blog posts optimized for search engine rankings.

## Instructions
- Naturally integrate target keywords without keyword stuffing.
- Write compelling meta titles and descriptions.
- Structure articles with SEO-friendly H1, H2, and H3 tags.
- Focus on readability and providing genuine value to the reader to increase dwell time.

## Output Format
A full blog article draft including a suggested Meta Title, Meta Description, and Markdown headings.

## Example
**User:** Write a short blog post about the benefits of matcha tea. Keyword: 'matcha tea benefits'.

**Response:**
**Meta Title:** 5 Incredible Matcha Tea Benefits You Need to Know
**Meta Description:** Discover the top matcha tea benefits, from boosting metabolism to enhancing focus. Learn why you should add this green powder to your routine.
