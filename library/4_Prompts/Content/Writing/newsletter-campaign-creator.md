# Newsletter Campaign Creator

## Purpose
This prompt drafts a cohesive sequence of engaging newsletter emails for marketing campaigns, product launches, or educational drip courses. It ensures consistent tone and strategic placement of calls to action across multiple touchpoints.

## Instructions
I want you to act as an expert email copywriter with a proven track record of high open and click-through rates.

Please create a [Enter Number, e.g., 3]-part newsletter email sequence for the following campaign topic: [Describe the topic or event, e.g., Black Friday SaaS Sale].
The desired tone of the emails is: [Enter Tone, e.g., Urgent, Exciting, Professional, Conversational].
The primary Call To Action (CTA) I want readers to take is: [Enter CTA, e.g., Click the link to claim a 50% discount].

For each email in the sequence, your response must include:
- A compelling Subject Line.
- Engaging Preview Text (the snippet seen in the inbox).
- The full body content of the email, structured for easy reading.
- Clear placement of the CTA.
- Advice on the delay/timing between this email and the next one.

## Output Format
- Use markdown formatting with clear headers separating Email 1, Email 2, etc.
- Format the subject lines and preview text distinctly from the body content.
- Use placeholders like `[First Name]` where personalization variables should be inserted by the email software.

## Example
**Input:**
Number: 2
Topic: Announcing a new webinar on SEO Basics.
Tone: Educational and enthusiastic.
CTA: Register for the free webinar via the provided link.

**Output:**
## Email 1: The Announcement
**Subject:** Stop guessing with SEO. Learn the basics live with us. 📈
**Preview Text:** Join our free webinar and get your website ranking higher on Google.

Hi [First Name],

Are you tired of publishing great content that nobody reads?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) doesn't have to be a dark art. In fact, just mastering a few foundational basics can drastically improve your website's visibility.

That's why we're hosting a **Free Live Webinar: SEO Basics for Beginners** next Tuesday at 10 AM EST.

We'll cover:
- How to find keywords your audience is actually searching for.
- The 3 on-page tweaks you need to make to every article.
- How to get Google to index your pages faster.

Space is limited, so secure your spot today!

[Link: Register for the Free Webinar Here]

See you there,
The Team

*Delay advice: Send Email 2 exactly 24 hours before the webinar begins.*

## Email 2: The Reminder
**Subject:** Starting tomorrow: SEO Basics Webinar ⏳
**Preview Text:** We go live in 24 hours! Have you secured your spot yet?

Hi [First Name],

This is a quick reminder that our live webinar on SEO Basics kicks off tomorrow at 10 AM EST!

If you want to learn how to drive consistent, organic traffic to your site without paying for ads, you won't want to miss this.

[Link: Click here to claim your spot before registration closes]

Bring a notebook and your questions, because we're doing a live Q&A at the end.

Cheers,
The Team

## Tips for Use
- Provide specific details about any discounts, dates, or unique selling points to make the copy more persuasive.
- If you have an established brand voice, include a sentence describing it (e.g., "Use short sentences, no jargon, and include an emoji in every paragraph").
