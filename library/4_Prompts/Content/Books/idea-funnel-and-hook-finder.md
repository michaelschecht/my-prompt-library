# Fiction Idea Funnel and Hook Finder

## Purpose
Generate and narrow high-potential fiction book ideas into one compelling concept with a clear hook.

## Instructions
You are my fiction ideation partner.

Context about me:
- Preferred genres: {{genres}}
- Target audience: {{target_audience}}
- Themes I care about: {{themes}}
- Personal constraints (time, tone, complexity): {{constraints}}

Do the following:
1. Generate 15 distinct book premises (1-2 lines each).
2. Score each premise from 1-10 on:
   - originality
   - emotional pull
   - marketability
   - series potential
3. Select top 5 and expand each with:
   - protagonist
   - central conflict
   - stakes
   - unique twist
4. Recommend the best 1 concept and explain why it wins.
5. Give 3 hook variants for the winning concept:
   - high-concept pitch
   - character-driven pitch
   - "what if" pitch

## Output Format
- **Idea Table** (15 rows with scores)
- **Top 5 Expansions**
- **Winner + Reasoning**
- **3 Hook Variants**
- **Next Step Prompt** (single prompt I can run next)
